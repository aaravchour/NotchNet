Blocco is a puppy and minor character in Minecraft: Story Mode Season Two. He first appeared in "Below the Bedrock", and determinately gives a Fred fact by indirectly causing Willy to accidentally reveal the name of Fred's former pet chicken: Waffles.


== Appearance ==
Blocco is a unique wolf pup as he possesses a unique color coat of fur, having black fur, nose, eyes, tail, and ears, and has a white snout and paws. He also has a red tongue.


== Personality ==
Blocco is shown to be playful and loves to play fetch with people, like Jesse.


== Inventory ==
Bone


== Trivia ==
Blocco can hold items in it's moth similar to how a fox can. This is not seen in vanilla Minecraft wolves.
Blocco was a reference to Juan Vaca's - the lead designer for "Below the Bedrock" - dog named "Rocco."
