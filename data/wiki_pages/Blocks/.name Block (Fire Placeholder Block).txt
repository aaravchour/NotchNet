.name (AKA the Fire Placeholder Block) is a Block that was added in Update 0.3.3.


== Obtaining ==
Currently, it is only obtainable through Inventory Editing in Template:Mc.


== Usage ==
The .name Block served as a placeholder for Fire till Update 0.6.1, as in Update 0.7.0, Fire was re-added to the game.


== Trivia ==
The old texture was a Stone block.
Some Players have seen it as a block with all items, including unimplemented ones.
In Update 0.15.0, the .name block's texture was changed to the Info update's texture.
