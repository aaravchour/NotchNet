Chiseled tuff bricks is a chiseled variant of tuff bricks.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Chiseled tuff bricks drops as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Chiseled tuff bricks blocks generate naturally in trial chambers.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Other than being a decorative building block, chiseled tuff bricks has no other uses.
