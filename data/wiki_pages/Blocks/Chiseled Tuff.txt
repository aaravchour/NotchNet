Chiseled tuff is a chiseled variant of tuff.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Chiseled tuff drops as an item if mined by any pickaxe. If mined without a pickaxe, it drops nothing.


=== Natural generation ===
Chiseled tuff blocks naturally generate in trial chambers.


=== Crafting ===


=== Stonecutter ===
Chiseled tuff blocks can be obtained by using a normal tuff block on a Stonecutter.


== Trivia ==
Chiseled Tuff has intricate, winding spirals on both the top and the sides of the block. These spirals symbolize the Breeze's wind-charged attacks and the Wind Charged potion effects.
