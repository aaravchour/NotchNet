Resin Bricks (block) is a resin variant building block crafted from resin bricks (item).


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ===
Resin bricks can be used to craft resin bricks (block).


=== Cutting ===
Resin Bricks can be cut into their own different variant blocks via a stonecutter.


== AppearanceLike all resin variant blocks, Resin Bricks appear bright, contrasting orange, similar to lava. ==
