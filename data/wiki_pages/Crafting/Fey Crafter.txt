The Fey Crafter is a tile entity added by Roots 2. It is used to craft materials such as Rune Stones. It must be placed adjacent to an activated Grove Stone to function. Right-click the Crafter to open its crafting GUI and place the required materials.  Right-click with a Stone Knife or better to collect the items. Multiple sets of the same item can be crafted by proportionally adding components to the Crafter.
