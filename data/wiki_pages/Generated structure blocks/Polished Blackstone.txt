Polished blackstone is the stone-like polished variation of blackstone.
 Chiseled polished blackstone is the chiseled variation of polished blackstone.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===

Polished blackstone has a slightly higher hardness (thus takes slightly longer to break) than its chiseled variant.


=== Natural generation ===
Polished blackstone can generate as part of bastion remnants and ruined portals in the Nether.

== Trivia ==
Similarly to chiseled sandstone, red sandstone, nether bricks and deepslate, chiseled polished blackstone has a face/symbol in its texture - in this case, a piglin snout.
