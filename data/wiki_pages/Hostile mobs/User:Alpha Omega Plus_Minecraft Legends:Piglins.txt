Piglins are a species of hostile pig-like mobs from the Nether who have invaded the Overworld and are the main antagonists of Minecraft Legends.


== Overview ==
Piglins originate from the infernal realm of the Nether and came to the Overworld to conquer and spread corruption on its surface. They live in a structure society as they are all under the leadership of a grand chief and are divided into several hordes each led by a chief. All the hordes are distinct by both appearance and behavior, and have their own varieties of piglins.
