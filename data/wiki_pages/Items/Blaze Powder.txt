Blaze powder is an item made from a blaze rod, which is dropped by a blaze when killed. Its main uses are to fuel brewing stands, to brew strength potions, and to make eyes of ender to take the player to the End.

=== Crafting Ingredient ===


=== Brewing stand fuel ===
In addition to being a brewing ingredient, blaze powder is needed to fuel a brewing stand. Each piece of blaze powder can brew up to 20 batches of potions.
