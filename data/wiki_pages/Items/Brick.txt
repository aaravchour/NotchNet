A brick is an item used to craft brick blocks, flower pots, and decorated pots.


== Obtaining ==


=== Smelting ===
A brick can be obtained by smelting a clay ball.


=== Mining ===
When breaking a decorated pot with a tool without Silk Touch on the main hand, the decorated pot can drops 0-4 brick(s) depend on the material that the decorated pot make of.


=== Loot ===


=== Trading ===
Novice-level stone mason villagers sell 16‌ or 10‌ bricks for one emerald.


== Usage ==
Brick can be used to craft bricks, flower pots, and decorated pots.
