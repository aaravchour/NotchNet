A melon slice is a food item that can be eaten by the player.


== Obtaining ==


=== Block loot ===

Melon slices can be obtained by breaking (harvesting) melons using any tool or by hand. Axes, followed by swords, are the fastest tools for harvesting melons.  A melon drops 3â€“7 melon slices, when broken; by hand, using a tool, or after being broken by the movement of a piston, with an average of 4.64 slices per melon.
Breaking a melon with a tool enchanted with Fortune increases the potential number of drops by 1 slice per level, up to a maximum of 9 slices.


== Usage ==


=== Food ===

To eat a melon slice, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores 2 () hunger and 1.2 hunger 
saturation.


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Composting ===
Placing a melon slice into a composter has a 50% chance of raising the compost level by 1.
