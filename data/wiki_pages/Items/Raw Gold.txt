Raw gold is a raw metal resource obtained from mining gold ore.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mining ===
Gold ore or deepslate gold ore mined using an iron pickaxe or higher drops 1 unit of raw gold. If the pickaxe is enchanted with Fortune, it can drop an extra unit per level of Fortune, allowing for a maximum of 4 with Fortune III. If the ore is mined using a pickaxe enchanted with Silk Touch, it drops the ore block instead.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
The primary usage of raw gold is smelting it into gold ingots.


=== Crafting ===


=== Smelting ingredient ===


=== Piglins ===
Piglins are attracted to raw gold. They run toward any raw gold on the ground, and inspect it for 6 to 8 seconds before putting it in their inventory.
