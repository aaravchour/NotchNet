A slimeball is a crafting ingredient commonly dropped by slimes, and can be sneezed out by pandas.


== Obtaining ==


=== Mob loot ===


==== Slimes ====

If a slime's size is 1, it drops 0–2 slimeballs when killed. The maximum number of slimeballs is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 5 slimeballs with Looting III. When killed by a frog, they always drop 1 slimeball.


==== Pandas ====
Baby pandas have a 1⁄700 chance of dropping one slimeball when sneezing.


=== Crafting ===


=== Trading ===
Wandering traders sometimes offer to sell a slimeball for 4 emeralds. They only trade 4 per Trader.


== Usage ==


=== Crafting ingredient ===


=== Breeding ===
Slimeballs can be used to breed frogs and reduce the remaining growth duration of tadpoles by 10%. Both also follow a player holding a slimeball.
