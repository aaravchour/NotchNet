A copper torch is a torch variant that was added in The Copper Age. Like normal torches, it gives light when exposed to darkness.


== Obtaining ==


=== Breaking ===
Copper torches can be easily broken with any tool.

A copper torch also drop as an item if:

The attachment block is removed or destroyed.
Water or lava flows into it.
a piston tries to push it or moves a block into its space.


=== Crafting ===


== Usage ==
Like torches, copper torches can be placed on any solid blocks. It gives a light level of 14.
Unlike most copper blocks, copper torches do not oxidize.
