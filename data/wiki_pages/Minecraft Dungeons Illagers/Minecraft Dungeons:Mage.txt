A mage is an illager mob that can be found within the Arcade edition of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==

Mages resembles other illagers, specifically illusioners and iceologers, though mages wear long purple robes and have purple eyes. The points of the robe that hang in the front of a mage are lined with a dirty gold stripe along the edge. Mages appear to have a dark purple texture for the exposed shirt under the robe and a brown texture for pants that gets darker at the bottom. Unlike other illagers, mages have eyes and eyebrows that are part of its head texture, making the face static.


== Lore ==
A Bishop chess statue based on a mage was built in a rare room of the Lone Fortress.


== Spawning ==
Squid Coast (Arcade)


== Behavior ==

Strong attack
Mages are capable of levitating the hero up and then smashing them back down, but won't dealing any damage, The hero cannot attack when it is lifted by a mage.

Novelty

Mage planned "Novelty" animation. A similar animation plays in Minecraft Dungeons Arcade when the empowered mage teleports out.
