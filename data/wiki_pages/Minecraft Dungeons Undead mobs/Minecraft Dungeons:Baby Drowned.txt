A baby drowned is a hostile baby drowned variant that can be found within the missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The baby drowned resemble the drowned but are smaller and more agile.


== Spawning ==
Coral Rise
Abyssal Monument
Radiant Ravine
Treetop Tangle


== Behavior ==
Just like their land-based counterparts, they are capable of attacking the hero by running towards them and dealing small damage, and as such, they retreat after hitting the hero.
