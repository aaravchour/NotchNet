A mossy skeleton variant 1 & 2 is a mossy skeleton variant that can be found exclusively within the arcade edition of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
Mossy skeletons resemble normal mossy skeletons, but are slightly taller than average. They have noticeably more health due to their armor. They have two variants, with a power bow. They also wear a reinforced mail helmet and breastplate, or just a helmet. 


== Spawning ==
Dingy Jungle (Arcade)


== Behavior ==
Mossy skeletons are hostile towards heroes and will shoot them on sight, just like their normal counterparts, with the difference being unable to poison the hero even with their arrows.


== Melee mossy skeleton ==

A melee mossy skeleton is a defensive mossy skeleton variant that can be found exclusively within the arcade edition of Minecraft Dungeons.


=== Appearance ===
Melee mossy skeletons spawn wearing scale mail and sometimes the reinforced mail helmet giving them increased defense. Unlike ranged mossy skeletons though, they primarily use melee, utilizing swords and daggers instead of ranged.


=== Spawning ===
Dingy Jungle (Arcade)


=== Behavior ===
Like its normal variant, they attack the hero when seen but instead of ranged, they walk to the hero and attack them via melee.
