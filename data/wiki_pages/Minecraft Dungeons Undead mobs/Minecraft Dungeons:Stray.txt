A stray is a skeleton variant that can be found within the Creeping Winter DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==

== Behavior ==
Bow action
Strays behave the same as normal skeletons, but they shoot arrows that inflict the Freezing effect which will slow the hero down for a short time.

Walk
Strays flee when the hero gets near.

Novelty
Strays have a rare behavior when idle, in which they spin their skull rapidly as if playing with their heads. They have a sound file named "novelty spin" that plays when this behavior occurs.


== Minecraft Dungeons Arcade ==


=== Appearance ===
Strays in Arcade lack blue frostbitten bones and white eyes, and instead resemble a skeleton wearing the tattered clothes of strays. They wield a Bow rather than a Shortbow.
