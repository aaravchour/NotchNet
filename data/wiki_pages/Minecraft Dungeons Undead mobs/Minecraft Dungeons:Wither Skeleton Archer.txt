A wither skeleton archer is an undead wither skeleton variant that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
Wither skeleton archers look identical to wither skeletons, with the only difference being that they are equipped with a ranged weapon, the red snake.

== Behavior ==
Basic attack
Wither skeleton archers shoot targets from a distance with the red snake, firing black-colored arrows that inflict the Wither effect upon impact.
