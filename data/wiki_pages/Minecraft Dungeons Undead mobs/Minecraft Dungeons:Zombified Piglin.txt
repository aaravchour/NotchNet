A Zombified Piglin is a piglin variant that can be found within the Flames of the Nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Spawning ==
Nether Wastes
Warped Forest
Crimson Forest


== Appearance ==
Zombified Piglins in Minecraft Dungeons are similar in appearance to their counterpart within the original game with the slight exception that they are hostile. They are also in a hunched position unlike the original game.
Zombified piglins, like their normal counterparts have variants. The first variant wears a black armor set has one gold arm guard on its left arm, while the second variant wears a reddish armor set, has two gold guards on its arms, has a cracked Gold helmet. the first variants wields a gold axe while the second variant wields the Firebrand.


== Behavior ==
Zombified piglins in Minecraft Dungeons are completely hostile and attack the hero upon seeing them, attacking them with their gold sword. They are not immune to fire damage. They also don't scare nearby piglins unlike the original game.
