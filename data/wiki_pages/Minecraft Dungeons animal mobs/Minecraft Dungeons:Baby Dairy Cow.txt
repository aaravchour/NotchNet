The baby dairy cow is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The baby dairy cow is a juvenile version of the dairy cow albeit with altered or different spot patterns on its body.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub
Fauna Faire


== Behavior ==
The baby dairy cow follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
