The baby piebald pig is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The baby piebald pig is a juvenile version of the piebald pig with different brown spot placement around its body.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The baby piebald pig follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
