The brown tabby is a cosmetic pet mob that can be found in the upcoming Fauna Faire Seasonal Adventure.


== Appearance ==
The brown tabby is a variant of the cat with brown striped fur and white fur on its underbelly, face, and paws. It possesses yellow eyes and wears a red collar.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The brown tabby follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
