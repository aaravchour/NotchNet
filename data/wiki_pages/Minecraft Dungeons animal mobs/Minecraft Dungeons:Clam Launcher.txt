A clam launcher, is a non-attackable launchpad variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Spawning ==
Coral Rise
Abyssal Monument
Radiant Ravine


== Behavior ==
Clam launchers shake if a mob or hero is on top of it. After a few seconds of uninterrupted contact, it will launch the mob or hero into the air and then close. Some clams only act as an ambient prop resembling a clam launcher, in the files they are called Clam Prop and Cat Clam.

== Data values ==
Within the game files, clam launchers are referred to internally by the name Clam_Jumppad.
Within the game files, clams are referred to internally by the Clam_Prop.
Within the game files, cat clams are referred to internally by the name Clam_Easter_Egg.
