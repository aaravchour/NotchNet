The duck  is a pet mob revealed at Minecraft Live 2021 in Minecraft Dungeons, and added as a reward in the Cloudy Climb seasonal adventure. It resembles the baby chicken in appearance.


== Appearance ==
The duck is a bird mob that resembles the baby chicken in body style. It overall resembles the male mallard duck with the green head, white patches, and brown body.


== Obtaining ==
The duck can be obtained as a reward in the Cloudy Climb for the Tower.


== Behavior ==
The duck is purely cosmetic and does not attack enemies.

== Trivia ==
The duck being a mob may be a reference to Notch changing chickens to ducks around the end of the Java Edition Beta, hence why it took the body style of the baby chicken as a slight nod to this reference. However, it could also just be a coincidence.
