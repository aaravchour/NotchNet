The endermite is a cosmetic pet in Minecraft Dungeons obtained through the Echoing Void DLC. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack enemies.


== Appearance ==

The endermite resembles its hostile counterpart with the exception that it's passive and possesses a blue eye instead of a red eye. It also emits blue particles instead of purple ones.


== Obtaining ==
DLC
Echoing Void
Ultimate DLC Bundle
Season Pass (discontinued)
