A goat is a neutral animal that can be found within the howling peaks DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
Goats look mostly the same as they do within the original game, with the only difference being a darker texture and having their legs being a bit further away from the stomach.


== Spawning ==
Windswept Peaks
Gale Sanctum
Gauntlet of Gales
Colossal Rampart
Ancient Hunt


== Behavior ==
Attack
Goats wander idly around mountaintops retaliating against heroes if attacked first.

== Windbeard ==

Windbeard is an ancient goat that can be encountered during ancient hunts with the minimum offering of the following runes:

Windbeard is immediately hostile and attacks heroes without provocation. Windbeard resides within a Windswept Peaks-themed arena and possesses, at minimum, the following enchantments:

Double Damage
Fire Trail
Quick
Regeneration
Burning
Twenty enchanted mountaineers act as Windbeard's minions, possessing the following enchantments:

Regeneration
Upon defeating Windbeard, a gilded variant of the following items drops as a reward:

Climbing Gear
Goat Gear
Rugged Climbing Gear
Emerald Gear
Gilded Glory
Opulent Armor
Tempest Knife
Chill Gale Knife
Resolute Tempest Knife
Wind Bow
Burst Gale Bow
Echo of the Valley

== Trivia ==
A goat sound can be heard when hovering over or clicking on the Windswept Peaks map icon.

Normal non-enchanted mobs can also spawn within Windbeard's arena.
