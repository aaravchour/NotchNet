The golden axolotl is a cosmetic pet mob that can be found within the Luminous Night seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The golden axolotl is a golden yellow variant of the axolotl from the original game, possessing yellow fins and gills.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The wild axolotl follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
