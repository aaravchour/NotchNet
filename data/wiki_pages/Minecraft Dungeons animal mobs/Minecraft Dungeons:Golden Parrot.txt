The golden parrot is a golden parrot variant that can be unlocked as a reward in the Cloudy Climb seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The golden parrot is a gold colored variant of the parrot. As its name implies, its plumage is a glimmering gold with some feathers shining a pale yellow, it also possesses a dark gold beak and blue eyes.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==

The golden parrot is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
