A llama variant 1 also known as a llama is a neutral pet llama variant that can be found within the howling peaks DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
Llamas within the Howling Peaks do not have chests on their side and a carpet, although they retain the same brown coat color, and having the feet and face not covered in brown fur.


== Spawning ==
Windswept Peaks
Gale Sanctum
Gauntlet of Gales
Colossal Rampart


== Behavior ==
Llamas wander around aimlessly. Unlike pet llamas, these llamas are neutral and do not have carpets or chests on their backs. When a hero frees one from a cage, they perform a dance whenever they are freed from a cage. This behavior is seen in the cave where they are trapped within the windswept peaks.

Basic attack spit
Llama spit at targets, retaliating if attacked.
