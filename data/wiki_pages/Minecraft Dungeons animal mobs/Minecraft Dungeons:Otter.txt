The otter is an unused cosmetic pet mob that was planned for the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure but never made it outside two concepts.


== Appearance ==
The otter is a long sleek mob with brown fur and dirty white fur on its underbelly. It has dark paws and a big snout and nose. It lastly has a short thick tail It had another concept where it had whiskers.


== Obtaining ==


== Behavior ==
The otter follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
