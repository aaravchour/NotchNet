The red panda is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The red panda is a small mob with red fur covering its face and body and black bottom fur going around its legs and tip of its tail. It has white ears, cheeks, and a muzzle. It has a long bushy striped tail of the same color with lighter orange stripes.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub
Fauna Faire level 23 (free pass)


== Behavior ==
The red panda follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
