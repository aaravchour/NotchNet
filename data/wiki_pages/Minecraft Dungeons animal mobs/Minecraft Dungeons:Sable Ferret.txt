The sable ferret is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub
Fauna Faire level 40 (adventure pass)


== Behavior ==
The sable ferret follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
