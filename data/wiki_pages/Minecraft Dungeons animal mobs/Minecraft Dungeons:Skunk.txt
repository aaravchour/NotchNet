The skunk is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The skunk is a small mob with black fur with white patches on its side and tail. It has a small bushy tail keep upright, small legs, and a pink nose.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The skunk follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
