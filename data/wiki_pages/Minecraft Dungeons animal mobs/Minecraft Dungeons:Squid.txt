A squid is a passive mob that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Spawning ==
Coral Rise
Abyssal Monument
Radiant Ravine


== Behavior ==
Squids will harmlessly swim around the oceans, not really doing anything else. When attacked, they squirt ink which inflict the hero with the Blind effect for five seconds.
