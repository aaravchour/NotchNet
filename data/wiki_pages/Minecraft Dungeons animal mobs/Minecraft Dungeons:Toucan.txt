The toucan is a pet mob that was added to the Cloudy Climb seasonal adventure as an unlockable reward in Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==

The toucan is a small bird mob with its overall appearance similar to the common toucan with its signature yellow beak with a pink ridge, blue eyes, and black feathers.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The toucan is purely cosmetic and does not attack enemies.

== Trivia ==
It is the one of two brand new and unique bird mobs to be added in Dungeons, the second being the raven.
