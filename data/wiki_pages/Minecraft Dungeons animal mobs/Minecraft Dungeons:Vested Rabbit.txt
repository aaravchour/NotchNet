The vested rabbit is a cosmetic pet rabbit variant that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The vested rabbit is a rabbit variant with white fur, a gray patch of fur covering the face and up until the base of the ears. It also resembles the "Toast" rabbit skin. It’s lower body is light gray running down to it’s feet, which ends with a dark-gray patch. It has dark eyes.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The vested rabbit follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
