The wild axolotl is a cosmetic pet mob and axolotl variant that can be unlocked as a reward in the Luminous Night seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The wild axolotl is a wild variant of axolotls from the original game, possessing brown skin with few spots, limbs, and gills.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The wild axolotl swims through the air next to the hero.
