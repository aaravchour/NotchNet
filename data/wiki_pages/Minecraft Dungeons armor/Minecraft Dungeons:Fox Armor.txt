== Ancient villager ==
An ancient villager is an unseen mob that is mentioned within the item description of the fox armor of Minecraft Dungeons. The item description states that ancient villagers living in tribes made the fox armor in honor of a fox who was an agile and great warrior. It was made hard to pierce to put terror in the hearts of enemies.
