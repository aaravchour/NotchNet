Grim armor is an armor set that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the wither armor and, within the spookier fall event, the spooky gourdian.

== Usage ==
Grim Armor has life steal aura, which grants an amount of health to the hero based on the damage they deal (e.g. dealing 100 damage will grant six health to the hero assuming 6% life steal aura). Life steal is based on any type of damage (artifacts, melee, ranged, enchantments, etc.)


=== Properties ===
+X health
+50% souls gathered
6% life steal aura
