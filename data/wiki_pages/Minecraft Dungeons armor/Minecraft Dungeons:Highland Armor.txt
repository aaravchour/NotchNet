The highland armor is a unique scale mail variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Fiery Forge
Frosted Fjord (DLC)
Gauntlet of Gales
Basalt Deltas (DLC)

Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
First Enchanter
