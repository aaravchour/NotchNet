The hungriest horror is a unique mercenary armor variant that could be found within the Spooky Fall 2022 event of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The hungriest horror looks identical to the hungry horror, with the difference being that it is green rather than red.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall 2022 (Ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==
The hungriest horror functions identically to the renegade armor.


=== Properties ===
+X health
+25% melee attack speed
35% damage reduction
+20% weapon damage boost aura
