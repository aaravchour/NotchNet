Ocelot armor is an armor set that can be found within the jungle awakens DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the shadow walker.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Dingy Jungle
Overgrown Temple
Tower
Treetop Tangle
Merchants

Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Thundering Growth
