Piglin armor is an armor set that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the golden piglin armor.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.
