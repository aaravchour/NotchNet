The sturdy shulker armor is a unique shulker armor variant that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
End Wilds
Tower
Merchants
Gift Wrapper
Luxury Merchant
Mystery Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
The Swarm


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X health
Mobs target you more
Deflect enemy projectiles (Deflect)
Take less damage when swarmed (Thrive Under Pressure)
Fire shulker projectiles at nearby enemies
