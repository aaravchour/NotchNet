The sweet tooth is a unique spelunker armor variant that could be found within the various anniversaries of Minecraft Dungeons. It behaves identically to the cave crawler.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
First Anniversary (ended)
Second Anniversary (ended)
Other
Gift wrapper


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
+X health
+50% artifact damage
+20% weapon damage boost aura
Gives player a pet bat
