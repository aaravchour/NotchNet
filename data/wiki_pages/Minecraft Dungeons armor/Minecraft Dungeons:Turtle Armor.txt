Turtle armor is an armor set that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the nimble turtle armor.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Coral Rise
Radiant Ravine
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Vengeful Mariner

=== Properties ===

25% healing boost
