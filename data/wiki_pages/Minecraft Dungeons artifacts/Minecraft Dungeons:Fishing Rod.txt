The fishing rod is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Squid Coast
Soggy Swamp
Soggy Cave
Creepy Crypt
Frosted Fjord (DLC)
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
Pulls the closest mob in range to the hero's position, briefly stunning that mob. The stun duration scales with the power level of the item. When there are no mobs nearby, the fishing rod shoots out in front of the hero in a straight line before returning.


=== Properties ===
X second stun duration
3 second cooldown
