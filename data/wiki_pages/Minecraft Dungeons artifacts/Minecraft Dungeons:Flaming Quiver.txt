The flaming quiver is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Pumpkin Pastures
Fiery Forge
Nether Fortress (DLC)
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The flaming quiver grants the hero 7 flaming arrows when activated. Flaming arrows deal fire damage over time similar to the fire aspect enchantment.
The fire damage done by flaming arrows is increased by the Fire Focus enchantment.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damaged per second
30 seconds cooldown


=== Properties ===
Minimum damage for power level

== Trivia ==
The fire effect of the flaming quiver can be stacked with fire enchantments like fire aspect can be stacked and will do separate damage, and both effects mobs whether the arrow hits the mob or a mob close to them.
