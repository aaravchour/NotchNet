The harpoon quiver is an artifact that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Coral Rise
Abyssal Monument
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The harpoon quiver grants the hero 5 harpoons, which allow quick ranged shots underwater. This artifact similarly works like the flaming quiver, the torment quiver, the thundering quiver and the void quiver; with the exception of the fireworks arrow.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
30 second cooldown


== Harpoon ==

Harpoons are ammunition that can be obtained by activating the harpoon quiver. They travel quickly through water unlike regular arrows.
