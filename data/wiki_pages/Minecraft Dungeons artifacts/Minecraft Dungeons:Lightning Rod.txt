The lightning rod is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

== Usage ==
The lightning rod shoots lightning on a nearby mob, damaging it and the mobs around it. It deals a very large amount of damage in a small area.
The lightning rod has a two second cooldown, +1 soul gathering, and requires fifteen souls to activate. 
Equipment that increases Artifact Damage, such as the Souldancer Robe, Splendid Robe, and Cave Crawler affect the amount of damage dealt.
The damage range depends on the item's power.
