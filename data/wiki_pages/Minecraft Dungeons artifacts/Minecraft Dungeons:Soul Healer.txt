The soul healer is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Soggy Swamp
Soggy Cave
Fiery Forge
Overgrown Temple (DLC)
Crimson Forest (DLC)
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The amount of health healed is determined by the amounts of souls used to activate the artifact. Up to twenty souls (Fifty souls today) can be used at a time, with each soul being equal to the minimum healing value of the item.
The soul healer can be activated even when the hero is at full health.


=== Properties ===
X health healed
5 second cooldown
+1 Soul Gathering
Requires souls
