== Usage ==
The soul lantern, when used, will instantly summon a soul wizard, a creature made up from bound souls, which follows the hero and attacks mobs.


=== Properties ===
X summon damage
5 seconds cooldown
+1 soul gathering
Requires souls

== Soul wizard ==

The soul wizard is a summonable mob that can be summoned by the soul lantern within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


=== Appearance ===
The soul wizard is a rather small mob about the size of a vex or possibly smaller. Its body is covered in purple tattered clothing and wears a hat that resembles the upper part of a lantern. Its hands and face are only visible, with its hands and eyes seen glowing bright blue. On its waist is a red ribbon binding its clothing together. 


=== Spawning ===
The soul wizard can be summoned using the soul lantern which requires souls and will disappear a few seconds later.


=== Behavior ===
The soul wizard will follow the hero upon being summoned and will attack mobs that startle it. When the soul wizard is defeated, it will disappear with a shattering sound. Similar to the bees, the soul wizard has a limited survival time and will automatically disappear after a while, and the hero would need to use the soul lantern again to summon it.

Basic attack
Soul wizards attack by shooting soul bolts at mobs.

== Trivia ==
In the "Homecoming" achievements, The soul wizard is referred to by the name soul entity.
The soul wizard is the only summonable mob that is not based on any mob that can be found within the original game, making it unique to Minecraft Dungeons.
