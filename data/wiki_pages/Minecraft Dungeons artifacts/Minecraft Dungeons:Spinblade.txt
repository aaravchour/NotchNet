The Spinblade is an artifact in Minecraft Dungeons obtainable within the Flames of the Nether DLC.


== Obtaining ==
Locations
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Nether Wastes (Adventure)
Basalt Deltas
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
Upon activation the spinblade fires out in front of the hero, once a large distance has been reached it will stop and fly back to the hero. Any mob that is in the path of the spinblade will be damaged.


=== Properties ===
X artifact damage
5 second cooldown

== Trivia ==
In the game files, the Spinblade is referred to internally as the SpinWheel. It is also referred to internally in two files as the SpinWheelGadget.
Due to the Spinblade being a special projectile, it can destroy projectiles shot by hostile necromancers and the Nameless One.
