The tome of duplication is an artifact that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

End Wilds (Adventure)
Merchants

Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The tome of duplication functions similarly to the satchel of snacks and satchel of elixirs, where it is capable of creating a consumable item, with the unique chance of even creating it twice and even capable of making duplicates of non-food consumables, like TNT. The tome of duplication require forty souls to activate.
The tome of duplication cannot be used to duplicate conduits or elytra.


=== Properties ===
X% double item chance
10 second cooldown
+1 soul gathering
Requires souls
