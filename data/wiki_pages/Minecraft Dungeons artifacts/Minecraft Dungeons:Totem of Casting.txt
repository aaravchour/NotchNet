The totem of casting is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Redstone Mines
Fiery Forge
Lower Temple
Treetop Tangle
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The totem of casting uses seventy souls to create a large aura, after a short time an effect occurs where any hero standing in this aura has the cooldown of their artifacts reduced. However, in practice the cooldown is completely reset instead of just reduced. The range and size of this aura increases with the power level of the artifact.


=== Properties ===
X% range increase
10 second cooldown
+1 Soul gathering
Requires souls
