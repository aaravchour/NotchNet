The totem of regeneration is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Soggy Swamp
Soggy Cave
Fiery Forge
Dingy Jungle (DLC)
Basalt Deltas (DLC)
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The totem of regeneration can be activated temporarily on the ground, healing all allies within a four-block radius. It can also be used every twenty-five seconds for approximately five to ten seconds of healing.


=== Properties ===
X health healed
25 seconds cooldown
