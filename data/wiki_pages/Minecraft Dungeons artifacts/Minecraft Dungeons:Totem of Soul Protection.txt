The totem of soul protection is an Unused artifact within Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
The totem is currently unobtainable, and can only be obtained or used using third party software.


== Usage ==
The totem of soul protection works somewhat similarly to the Totem of Undying from the original game, where it creates a square space around the totem which revives the user and any allies who are within its vicinity.


=== Abilities ===
X health healed
1 second cooldown
+1 Soul Gathering
Requires Souls
