The wind horn is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Cacti Canyon
Pumpkin Pastures
Arch Haven
Overgrown Temple (DLC)
Windswept Peaks (DLC)
Nether Wastes (DLC)
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The wind horn emits a circular blast, pushing back mobs a certain distance away, and slowing them down briefly. If a mob is too close to the hero, it will not be pushed back (but will still be slowed down). The wind horn can light up non-attackable mobs that aren't affected by it.


=== Properties ===
X blocks pushed
10 second cooldown
