Bread is a food item found within Minecraft Dungeons. When consumed, it heals 100% over thirty seconds.


== Obtaining ==
Bread is mainly obtained by defeating mobs, and opening chests. 
It is also dropped from supply chests as the guaranteed food item. 
The common armor enchantment Food Reserves has a chance to create bread as well as other food items.
