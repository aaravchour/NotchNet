An oakwood brew is a consumable potion that can be found within the jungle awakens DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
It can be obtained as a drop by defeating mobs and opening chests.


== Usage ==
Drinking oakwood brews boost the hero's defense, allowing them to take reduced damage for fifteen seconds.
