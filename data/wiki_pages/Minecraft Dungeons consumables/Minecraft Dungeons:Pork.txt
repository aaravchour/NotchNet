Pork is a consumable food that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Pork is mainly obtained by defeating mobs and opening chests. 
The common Food Reserves armor enchantment has a chance to create pork, as well as other food items.
Hoglins drop a large amount of pork when defeated.


== Usage ==
When consumed, pork heals 50% of the hero's max health over a period of ten seconds.
