The baby pig is a cosmetic pet mob that can be summoned within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. It was released on February 24, 2021 to all heroes as part of a free update to commemorate 10 million Dungeons players.


== Appearance ==
The baby pig is similar to the baby pig in the original game, with its disproportionately large head compared to its body.


== Behavior ==
The baby pig is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
