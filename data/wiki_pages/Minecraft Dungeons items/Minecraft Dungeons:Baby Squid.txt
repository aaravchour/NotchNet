The baby squid is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Cloudy Climb seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The baby squid is a baby glow squid with a body style similar to that of the baby ghast. It possesses small glowing tentacles, small green eyes, and two spots on its head.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The baby squid is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
