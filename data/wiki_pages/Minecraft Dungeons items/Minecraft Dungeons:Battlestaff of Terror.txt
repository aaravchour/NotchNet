== Usage ==
The battlestaff of terror possesses moderate power, low speed, and moderate range. Its attack combo consists of two side swings, one wide swing, one slam, two stabbing moves, two side twirls, one spin attack, and one powerful slam. Its combo resets after 0.5 seconds, unlike most other weapons which reset their combos after three seconds.


=== Properties ===
 X melee damage
 Stylish combo
