The blast fungus is an artifact that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC, of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Warped Forest (Adventure)
Crimson Forest
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
When used, the blast fungus sprout out, spread in different directions, and explode, dealing damage to any target within its range. It acts similarly to the MCD:Scatter Mines, with the exception that they detonate faster.


=== Properties ===
X artifact damage
6 second cooldown
