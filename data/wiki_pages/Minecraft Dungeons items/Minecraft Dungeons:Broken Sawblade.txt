A broken sawblade is a melee weapon that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the mechanized sawblade.

== Usage ==
Broken sawblades possess low power, high speed, and low area. They have a combo consisting of rapid damage until overheating. The hero can manually end the combo by letting go of the attack button.
Broken sawblades have unique behavior involving combos, where once they have been used for a certain amount of time, they overheat. This means they cannot be used for a short time, and an icon appears on the screen above the status affects area indicating this.
