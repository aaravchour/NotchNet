The call of the void is a unique void bow variant that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Lore ==
The item description states that beyond the sound of the void, there is another unknown voice in the darkness.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Broken Citadel
Tower
Merchants
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Scuttling Torment


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Applies rising damage multiplier (Void Strike tier I)
Chance for arrows to explode (Fuse Shot tier I)


=== Stats ===
Charge Multiplier: 1.6
Quiver: 53
Fire Rate: 1.8 shots per second (uncharged)
