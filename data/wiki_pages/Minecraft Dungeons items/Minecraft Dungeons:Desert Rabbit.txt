The desert rabbit is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The desert rabbit is a brown and sandy variant of the rabbit, with sandy fur and brown eyes.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The desert rabbit follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
