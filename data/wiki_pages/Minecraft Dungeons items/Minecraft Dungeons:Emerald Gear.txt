Emerald gear is an armor set that can be found within the howling peaks DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It has two unique variants, the opulent armor and the gilded glory.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Gale Sanctum
Colossal Rampart (Adventure)
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Windbeard
