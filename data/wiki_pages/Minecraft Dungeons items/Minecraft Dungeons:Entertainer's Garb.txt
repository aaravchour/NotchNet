An entertainer's garb is an armor set that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It has a unique variant called the troubadour.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
The Stronghold
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
+X health
Health potions heal nearby allies
+30% positive status effect duration
