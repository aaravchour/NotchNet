The eternal knife is a unique soul knife variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Soggy Swamp (Adventure)
Desert Temple
Lone Fortress (Adventure, DLC)
Tower
Merchants
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
The Seeking Flame


== Usage ==
The eternal knife possesses high power, low speed, and low range, and attacks only with single thrusts.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Thrust attack
Chance to Gain Souls (Soul Siphon tier I)
+2 Soul gathering


=== Stats ===

In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
