An eye of ender is a consumable that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Mobs
Savage Eye
Pumpkin Pastures
Ravenous Eye
Highblock Halls
Blight Eye
Soggy Swamp
Reaping Eye
Desert Temple
Binding Eye
Cacti Canyon
Spiked Eye
Creeper Woods


== Usage ==
The eyes of ender can be used to open the eye of ender doors that are located within the stronghold on the island near the mainland. They can also be used to open the End portal in order to traverse into the End.
