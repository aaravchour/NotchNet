The firebolt thrower is a unique exploding crossbow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

== Usage ==
The firebolt thrower possesses very high power along with burning exploding arrows, low speed, and a very low ammo supply.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Explodes on Impact
Chance of chain reaction (Chain Reaction tier I)


=== Stats ===
Multiplier: 1.2
Quiver: 20
Fire Rate: 1 shot per second
In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
