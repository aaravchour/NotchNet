The full metal armor is the unique plate armor that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

== Usage ==
The full metal armor can protect against damage and provide the hero with numerous bonus stats. Like plate armor, it makes the hero have a longer roll cooldown in exchange for much heavier defense.


=== Properties ===
+X health
+30% melee damage
30% chance to negate hits
35% damage reduction
100% increased rolling cooldown
