The gong of weakening is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The gong of weakening is a grey-purple gong with a light grey centre made out of some kind of metal material. It has two red ropes holding it up attached to the back. A black mallet with a brown handle is also seen next to the gong of weakening.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
When used, it weakens enemies in a 7-block radius around the hero, making them take 2 times more damage and applies the Weakening effect to them. The duration of this effect depends on the power level of the item.
