A great hammer is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the hammer of gravity, the stormlander, and the bonehead hammer.


== Appearance ==
The great hammer is a very large grey hammer with a wooden handle. The hammer looks slightly deteriorated on the mallet, and has a small section of light grey at the base of the handle.

== Usage ==
Great hammers possess high power, low speed, and high range. They have a combo consisting of single strong strikes.
