The grey tabby is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The grey tabby is a variant of the cat with dark grey and striped fur, as well as white fur on its underbelly, paws, and face. It possesses blue eyes and wears a red collar.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The grey tabby follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
