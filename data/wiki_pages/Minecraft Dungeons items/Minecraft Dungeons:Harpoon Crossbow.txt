A harpoon crossbow is a ranged weapon that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It has a unique variant called the nautical crossbow.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Abyssal Monument
Radiant Ravine
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Abyssal Eye


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Fires harpoons


=== Stats ===
Quiver: 53
Fire Rate: 2.5 shots per second
