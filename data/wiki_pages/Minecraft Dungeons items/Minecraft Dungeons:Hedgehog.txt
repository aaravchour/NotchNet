The hedgehog is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The hedgehog is a small round mob with brown fur which represent its spikes and a white body, with a brown nose and black eyes. It has small limbs and a tongue sticking out.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub
Fauna Faire level 50 (adventure pass)


== Behavior ==
The hedgehog follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs. It has a unique walk animation wherein it rolls around.
