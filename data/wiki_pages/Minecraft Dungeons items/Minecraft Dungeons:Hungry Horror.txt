The hungry horror is a unique mercenary armor variant that could be found within the various spooky fall missions of Minecraft Dungeons. It behaves identically to renegade armor.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall (Ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==
The hungry horror provides a melee attack speed boost as well as increased attack damage and damage reduction.


=== Properties ===
+X health
+25% melee attack speed
+35% damage reduction
+20% weapon damage boost aura


=== Stats ===
Minimum health expected for power level
