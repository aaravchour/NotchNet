The ice wand is an artifact that can be found within the creeping winter DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Frosted Fjord
Lone Fortress
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Properties ==
X artifact damage
Stuns Mobs
15 seconds cooldown
