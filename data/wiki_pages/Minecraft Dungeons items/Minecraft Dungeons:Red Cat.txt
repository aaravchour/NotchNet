The red cat is a cosmetic pet mob that can be found in the Fauna Faire Seasonal Adventure.


== Appearance ==
The red cat is a variant of the cat possessing gold-orange fur, green eyes, and white patches on its tail, paws, and underbelly.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The red cat follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
