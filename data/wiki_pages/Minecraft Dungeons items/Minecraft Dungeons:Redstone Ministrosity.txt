The redstone ministrosity is a cosmetic pet golem that can be unlocked as a reward in the Luminous Night seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The redstone ministrosity appears to be a tiny version of the redstone monstrosity with its overall appearance similar in size to the Copper Golem from Minecraft Live 2021, having patches of redstone on it.


== Obtaining ==
Adventure Hub


== Behavior ==
The redstone ministrosity is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.

== Data values ==
In the game files, the redstone ministrosity is referred to internally by the name BabyRedstoneMonstrosity.
