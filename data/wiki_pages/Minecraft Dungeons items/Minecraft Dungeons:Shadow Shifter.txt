The shadow shifter is an artifact that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
End Wilds
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant


== Usage ==
The shadow shifter can be used to hide from enemies as it can grant the hero shadow form for a substantial amount of time upon activation.


=== Properties ===
X second duration
10 second cooldown
+1 soul gathering
Requires souls (40)

== Data values ==
