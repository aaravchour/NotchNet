== Black ocelot ==

A black ocelot is an unseen mob mentioned within the item description of the shadow walker armor of Minecraft Dungeons. Black ocelots were legendary, graceful, and deadly mobs that left their enemies in their shadow. It is implied that black ocelots look similarly to the texture of the shadow walker armor.
