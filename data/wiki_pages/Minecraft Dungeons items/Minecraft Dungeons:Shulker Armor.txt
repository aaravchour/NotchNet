Shulker armor is an armor set that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It has a unique variant called the sturdy shulker armor.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
End Wilds
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
The Swarm


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X health
Mobs target you more often
Deflect enemy projectiles (Deflect tier I)
Take less damage when swarmed (Thrive Under Pressure).
