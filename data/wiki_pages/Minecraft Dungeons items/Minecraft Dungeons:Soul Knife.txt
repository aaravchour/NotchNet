A soul knife is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the eternal knife and the truthseeker.

== Usage ==
A soul knife possesses high power, low speed, and low range, and can attack only with single thrusts.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Thrust attack
+1 Soul gathering

== Trivia ==
Soul Knife is likely reference to Kris, a dagger from Indonesia. Similar to the description of the Soul Knife, Kris is believed to have spiritual power.
