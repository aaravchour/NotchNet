Sweet berries are a consumable food that can be found within the howling peaks DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==


== Usage ==
Sweet berries can heal over 20% of the hero's health and boosts their speed for five seconds.


== Sounds ==


== History ==


== Trivia ==
Sweet berries can be found in abundance growing on bushes within the camp and within the squid coast, though they do not deal damage or reduce the speed of heroes.
