Teleportation robes are an armor set that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the unstable robes.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Broken Citadel
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Scuttling Torment

=== Properties ===
+X health
Roll to teleport
+50% souls gathered
