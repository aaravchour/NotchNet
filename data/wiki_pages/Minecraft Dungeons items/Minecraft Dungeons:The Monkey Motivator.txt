The monkey motivator is an unused unique pickaxe variant that was scrapped during the development of the Jungle Awakens DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Adding the monkey motivator to the inventory with an editor will crash the game when clicked on.


== Monkey ==

A monkey is an unseen mob mentioned within the item name of the monkey motivator.
