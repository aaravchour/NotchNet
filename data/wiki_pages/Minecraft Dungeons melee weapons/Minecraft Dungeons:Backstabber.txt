A backstabber is a melee weapon that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is called the swift striker.


== Lore ==
The item description states that rogues such as thieves and assassins prefer to use backstabbers in their pack over any other blade.

== Usage ==
Backstabbers possess low power, moderate speed, and low area. They have a combo consisting of two quick strikes, and one stronger strike. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum damage, and the third hit deals the weapon's maximum damage.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Extra damage on unsuspecting enemies (Ambush tier I)
