A battlestaff is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the battlestaff of terror and the growing staff.

== Usage ==
Battlestaffs possesses moderate power, low speed, and moderate range. Its attack combo consists of two side swings, one wide swing, one slam, two stabbing moves, two side twirls, one spin attack, and one powerful slam. Its combo resets after 0.5 seconds, unlike most other weapons which reset their combos after three seconds.
