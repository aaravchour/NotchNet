The cackling broom is a unique glaive variant found within the Spookier Fall event of Minecraft Dungeons. It has identical abilities to the grave bane.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
Spookier Fall (ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==
The cackling broom functions identically to the grave bane.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Longer melee reach
Extra damage to undead (Smiting tier I)

In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
