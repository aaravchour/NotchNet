A coral blade is a melee weapon that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the sponge striker.

== Usage ==
Coral blades possess low power, moderate speed, and low area. They have a combo consisting of two side slashes, and then one upwards slash which deals the weapon's maximum melee damage.
