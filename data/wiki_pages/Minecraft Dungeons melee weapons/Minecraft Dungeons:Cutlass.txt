A cutlass is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the nameless blade, the dancer's sword, and the sparkler.

== Usage ==
A cutlass possesses low power, low speed, moderate area, and has a continuous attack of two sideways slashes.


=== Properties ===

Reliable combo
