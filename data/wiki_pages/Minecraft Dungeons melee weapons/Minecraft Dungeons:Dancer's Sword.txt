== Usage ==
The dancer's sword possesses moderate power, low speed, moderate area, and has a continuous attack of two sideways slashes. The built-in Rampaging enchantment gives a 10% to increase attack speed by 50% for 5 seconds after defeating a mob.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Reliable Combo
Increased attack speed (Rampaging tier I)
