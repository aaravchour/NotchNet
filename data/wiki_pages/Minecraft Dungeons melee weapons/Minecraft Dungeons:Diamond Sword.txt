The diamond sword is a unique sword variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The diamond sword is almost same as they are in the main Minecraft game, with a teal blade and broad design, with the only difference being that the color is brighter and it has more light reflections.

== Usage ==
The diamond sword possesses moderate power, low speed, and low range. It has a combo consisting of two side slashes and a thrust. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, while the thrust deals the maximum melee damage.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Extra damage (Sharpness tier I)
