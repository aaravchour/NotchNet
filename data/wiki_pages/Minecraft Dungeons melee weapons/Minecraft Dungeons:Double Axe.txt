A double axe is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons, wielded by some vindicators. Its unique variants are the whirlwind and the cursed axe.


== Appearance ==
The double axe is a large axe with a double sided head. It features a wooden handle with a red material where the hero would hold it.

== Usage ==
Double axes possess high power, low speed, and high range, and attack only with spins.
