The freezing foil is a unique rapier variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
A  after a mission name indicates that the item is only obtainable in Daily Trials of that mission.

Merchants

Ancients
Solemn Giant


== Usage ==
The freezing foil possesses low power, high speed, and above average area. It has a combo consisting of 13 fast thrusts, and then a large thrust that deals the weapon's maximum damage.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Fast thrusts
Slows mobs (Freezing tier I)
