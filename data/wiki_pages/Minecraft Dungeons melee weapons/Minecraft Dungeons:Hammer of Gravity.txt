The hammer of gravity is a unique great hammer variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

== Usage ==
The hammer of gravity possess high power, low speed, and high range. They have a combo consisting of single strong strikes.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Great splash
Pulls in enemies (Gravity tier I)
