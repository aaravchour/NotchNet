The highland axe is a unique axe variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The highland axe appears to be half of the whirlwind double axe, but with its blade facing upward.

== Usage ==
The highland axe possesses moderate power, low speed, and moderate area. It has a combo consisting of two slashes and one stronger spin attack. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum damage, and the third hit deals the weapon's maximum damage.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Spin attack
Stuns mobs (Stunning tier I)
