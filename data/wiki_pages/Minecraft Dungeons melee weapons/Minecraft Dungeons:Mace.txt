A mace is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons, as well as wielded by royal guards and guardian vexes. Its unique variants are called the sun's grace and the flail.


== Appearance ==
The Mace features a grey, red, and brown handle that connects to a grey cube with a single spike coming out of each side. It is a short hand held weapon.

== Usage ==
Maces possess above average power, low speed, and low range. They have a combo consisting of two side swings and an overhead slam. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum damage, while the overhead slam deals the weapon's maximum damage.
