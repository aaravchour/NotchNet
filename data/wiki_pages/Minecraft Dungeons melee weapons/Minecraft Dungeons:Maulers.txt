== Usage ==
The maulers possess low power, high speed, and low area, and has a combo consisting of four alternating punches, a left hook, an uppercut, and a lunge punch. The first five hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, the uppercut does around 40% more damage, and the lunge punch deals the maximum melee damage.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Relentless combo
Increased attack speed (Rampaging tier I)

== Ancient illager soldier ==
An ancient illager soldier is an unseen illager, referred to as the wielders of the maulers. Little is known about their appearance other than that they mostly wear the maulers and that they are ancient.
