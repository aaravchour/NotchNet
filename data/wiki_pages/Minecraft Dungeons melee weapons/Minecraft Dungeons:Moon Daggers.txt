The moon daggers are a unique daggers variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions

The Tiny Scourge


== Usage ==
The moon daggers possess low power, high speed, and low area, and has a continuous attack of six sideways slashes.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Dual wield
Souls critical boost (Enigma Resonator tier I)
+2 soul gathering
