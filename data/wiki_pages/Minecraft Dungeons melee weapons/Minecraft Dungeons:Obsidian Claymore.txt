The Obsidian Claymore is a melee weapon that can be found within the Echoing Void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It has a unique variant called The Starless Night.

== Usage ==
The Obsidian Claymore possesses above average power, low speed, and high area. Its combo consists of a slash which deals the weapon's minimum damage, and a slam which deals the weapon's maximum damage.
