A rapier is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the bee stinger and the freezing foil.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
A  after a mission name indicates that the item is only obtainable in Daily Trials of that mission.

== Usage ==
Rapiers possess low power, high speed, and above average area. They have a combo consisting of 13 fast thrusts, and then a large thrust that deals the weapon's maximum damage.
