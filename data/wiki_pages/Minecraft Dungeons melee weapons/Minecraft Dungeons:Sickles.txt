Sickles are melee weapons that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the last laugh and the nightmare's bite.

== Usage ==
Sickles possess low power, moderate speed, and low range. They have a combo consisting of four alternating quick strikes, and two stronger strikes. The first four hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, the fifth does 100% more damage, and the sixth deals the maximum melee damage.
