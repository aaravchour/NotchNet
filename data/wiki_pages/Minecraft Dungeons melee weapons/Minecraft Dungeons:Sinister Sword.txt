The sinister sword is a unique sword variant that could be found within the various spooky fall missions of Minecraft Dungeons. It behaves identically to the hawkbrand.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall (ended)
Merchant
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==
The sinister sword possesses moderate power, low speed, and low range. It has a combo consisting of two side slashes and a thrust. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, while the thrust deals the maximum melee damage. 


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Gives critical hit chance (Critical Hit tier I)
