A spear is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the fortune spear, whispering spear and the spine-chill spear. It has an unobtainable variant called the tower guard spear, wielded by tower guards.

== Usage ==
Spears possess moderate power, low speed, and high range, and have a combo consisting of three thrusts. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, the third deals the maximum melee damage.
