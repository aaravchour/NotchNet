The spine-chill spear is a unique spear variant that could be found within the Spooky Fall 2022 event of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall 2022 (ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==
The spine-chill spear functions identically to the whispering spear.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Long melee reach
Sometimes strikes twice (Echo tier I)
