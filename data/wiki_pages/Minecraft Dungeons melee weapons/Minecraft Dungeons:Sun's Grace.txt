The sun's grace is a unique mace variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The sun's grace is the same size as the mace but with a golden colour instead of grey, the "ball" of the mace is also emanating light.

== Usage ==
The sun's grace possesses above average power, low speed, and low range. It has a combo consisting of two side swings and an overhead slam. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum damage, while the overhead slam deals the weapon's maximum damage.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Heals allies in the area (Radiance tier I)
