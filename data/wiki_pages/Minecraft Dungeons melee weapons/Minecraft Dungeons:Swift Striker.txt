The swift striker is a unique backstabber variant that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
End Wilds
Tower
Merchants
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
The Swarm


== Usage ==
The swift striker possesses low power, moderate speed, and low area. It has a combo consisting of two quick strikes, and one stronger strike. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum damage, and the third hit deals the weapon's maximum damage.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Extra damage on unsuspecting enemies (Ambush tier I)
Sometimes attacks twice (Echo tier I)
