A whip is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the vine whip.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Dingy Jungle
Panda Plateau
Treetop Tangle
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Thundering Growth


== Usage ==
Whips possess high power, low speed, and low area, and attack only with one lash.


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Longer melee reach
