== Usage ==
The whispering spear possesses moderate power, low speed, and high range, and has a combo consisting of three thrusts. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, the third deals the maximum melee damage. Every 5 seconds the built in Echo enchantment activates and strikes twice.  


=== Properties ===
X melee damage
Long melee reach
Sometimes strikes twice (Echo tier I)
