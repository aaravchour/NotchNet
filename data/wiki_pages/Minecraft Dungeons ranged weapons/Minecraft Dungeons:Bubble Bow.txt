A bubble bow is a ranged weapon that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the bubble burster and gloopy bow.


== Obtaining ==

Vengeful Mariner


== Usage ==
A bubble bow has the ability to trap mobs in bubbles via charged shots that damage and eventually defeat them.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Bubble damage


=== Stats ===
Charge Multipler: 2.5
Charge Time: 0.35 seconds
Quiver: 80
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second (uncharged)
