The bubble burster is a unique bubble bow variant that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Coral Rise
Abyssal Monument
Tower
Merchants
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Vengeful Mariner


== Usage ==
The bubble burster can be used to trap mobs in bubbles via charged shots that eventually defeat them. The bubble burster has a unique exclusive enchantment which allows charged shots to bounce off multiple targets.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Bubble damage
Hits multiple targets when charged (Reliable Ricochet)


=== Stats ===
Charge Multipler: 2.5
Charge Time: 0.35 seconds
Quiver: 80
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second (uncharged)
