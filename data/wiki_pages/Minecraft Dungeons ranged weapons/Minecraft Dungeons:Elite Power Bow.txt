The elite power bow is a  unique power bow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons, wielded by some sunken skeletons.

== Usage ==
The Elite Power Bow has very high power, low speed, and low ammo.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Strong Charged Attacks
Greater damage (Power tier I)


=== Stats ===
Chaege Multiplier: 3
Quiver: 26
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second (uncharged)
In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
