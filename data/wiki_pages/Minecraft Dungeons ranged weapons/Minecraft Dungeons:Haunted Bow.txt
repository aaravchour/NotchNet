The haunted bow is a unique Bow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. It behaves identically to the Twin Bow.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall (Ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==
The haunted bow functions identically to the twin bow.


=== Properties ===
X Ranged Damage
Shoot two enemies at once (Bonus Shot tier I)


=== Stats ===
Charge Multiplier: 2.5 
Quiver: 80 
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second (uncharged)
