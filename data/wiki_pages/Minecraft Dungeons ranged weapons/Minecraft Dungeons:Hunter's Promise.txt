The hunter's promise is a unique hunting bow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The Hunter's Promise is similar in appearance to the hunting bow except the handle and string have a brown tint to them. It also features a white feather attached to the top section of the bow.
