The purple storm is a unique shortbow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Desert Temple

The Stronghold (DLC)

Ancients
Oozing Menace


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Increased fire rate (Rapid Fire tier I)


=== Stats ===
Charge Multiplier: 1.65
Quiver: 160
Fire Rate: 4 shots per second (uncharged)
In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
