A shadow crossbow is a ranged weapon that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It's unique variant is called the veiled crossbow.


== Appearance ==
A shadow crossbow is lilac bow with vibrant blue strings. It has a grey tip with a sturdy handle, and white white spots along the edge of the bow. There are what appear to be three purple gemstones encrusted in the handle. Maybe these are the source of this crossbow's shadow form abilities...
