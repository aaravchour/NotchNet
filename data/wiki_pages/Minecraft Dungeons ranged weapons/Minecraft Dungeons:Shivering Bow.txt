The shivering bow is a unique soul bow variant that can be found within the various seasonal trials of Minecraft Dungeons.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
Chills and Thrills (ended)
Festival of Frost (ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Steals speed (Tempo Theft tier I)
+1 soul gathering


=== Stats ===
Quiver: 66
Charge Multiplier: 2.5
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second
