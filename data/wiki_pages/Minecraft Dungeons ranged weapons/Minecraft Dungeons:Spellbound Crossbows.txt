The spellbound crossbows is a unique dual crossbows variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. It is one of the rarest ranged weapons in the game.

== Usage ==
The spellbound crossbows acquire moderate power, speed, and ammo capacity.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Double Projectiles
Strong vs Enchanted Mobs (Unchanting tier I)


=== Stats ===
Quiver: 46
Fire Rate: 1.44 shots per second (2.88 projectiles per second)
