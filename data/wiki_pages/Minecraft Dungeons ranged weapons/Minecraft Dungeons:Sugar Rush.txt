The sugar rush is a unique trickbow variant that can be found within the various anniversary missions of Minecraft Dungeons. It behaves identically to the pink scoundrel.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Tower
Events
First Anniversary (ended)
Second Anniversary (ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Hits multiple targets (Ricochet tier I)
Chance to enrage mobs (Wild Rage tier I)


=== Stats ===
Charge Multiplier: 2.2
Quiver: 66
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second (uncharged)
In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
