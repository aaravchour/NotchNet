The green menace is a unique trickbow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

== Usage ==
The green menace has low power, low speed and moderate ammunition. The quiver starts with 66 arrows.


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Hits multiple targets (Ricochet tier I)
Spawns poison clouds (Poison Cloud tier I)


=== Stats ===
Charge Multiplier: 2.2
Quiver: 66
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second (uncharged)
In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
