A void bow is a ranged weapon that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is called the call of the void.


== Obtaining ==
Missions
Broken Citadel
Tower
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Scuttling Torment


== Usage ==


=== Properties ===
X ranged damage
Applies Rising Damage Multiplier (Void Strike tier I)


=== Stats ===
Charge Multiplier: 1.6
Quiver: 53
Fire Rate: 1.8 shots per second (uncharged)
