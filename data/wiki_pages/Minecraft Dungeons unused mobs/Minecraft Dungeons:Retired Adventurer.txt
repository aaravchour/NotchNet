The Retired Adventurer is an unused Camp Merchant within Minecraft Dungeons.


== Appearance ==
The retired adventurer is a merchant with a special outfit, sporting a green and red robe with gold patches and wears a bandanna. This merchant also wore a golden crown with a red jewel on it as well as sandals. The quest giver resembles the Savanna villagers from the original game.
