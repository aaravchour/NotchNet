A desert rabbit was a cream variant of the rabbit, found only in Minecraft Earth.


== Appearance ==
Desert rabbits were a cream variant of the rabbit. The entirety of its fur was cream. Its nose was pink and its eyes were dark brown in color.


== Behavior ==

Similar to ordinary rabbits, desert rabbits had the same AI behavior, wandering aimlessly, and avoiding lava and cliffs that were enough to cause fall damage.


== Obtaining ==
Desert rabbits were an uncommon mob mob drop found in tappables.


== Drops ==
When killed, desert rabbits dropped:

0-1 Rabbit hide
0-1 Raw Rabbit (Cooked Rabbit if killed while on fire)
Rabbit's foot (10% chance of dropping)
