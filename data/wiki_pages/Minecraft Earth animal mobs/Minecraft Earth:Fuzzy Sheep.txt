The fuzzy sheep was a fuzzy and very wooly variant of the sheep found only in Minecraft Earth.


== Appearance ==
Fuzzy sheep were a very wooly variant of the sheep. Their fur resembled that of a wooly cow's pelt and was seen hanging down on its legs. They possessed black legs, a dark brown face, and white hooves, as well as a small tongue that stuck out below its nose.


== Behavior ==

Similar to ordinary sheep, fuzzy sheep had the same AI behavior, wandering aimlessly, avoiding lava and cliffs high enough to cause fall damage.


== Obtaining ==
Fuzzy sheep were an uncommon mob drop found in sheep tappables. They were also obtainable as a reward in Season 17.


== Drops ==
When sheared, fuzzy sheep dropped 1-6 White Wool.
When killed, fuzzy sheep dropped:

1 White Wool
1 Raw Mutton (Cooked Mutton if killed while on fire)
