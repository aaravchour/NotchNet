The hyper rabbit was a hyper variant of the rabbit that appeared in Minecraft Earth concept art.


== Appearance ==
The hyper rabbit appears to be a yellowish-colored rabbit with teal eyes, long whiskers, uneven fur, and lopsided ears. Based on the name, it is likely that its behavior may have been "hyper" in some way.
