The pinto cow was a cream and brown-colored variant of the cow found only in Minecraft Earth.


== Appearance ==
Pinto cows were a cream and brown-colored variant of the cow. They had cream-colored fur with large brownish patches all throughout the body. They had light-brown eyebrows and horns with white tips and a faint pink muzzle. They also possessed light-brown hooves.


== Behavior ==

Similar to normal cows, pinto cows wandered around, mooing, and breathing occasionally. They avoided water, lava, and walking off cliffs high enough to cause fall damage. They fled for a few seconds when harmed.


== Obtaining ==
The pinto cow was an uncommon mob drop found in cow tappables. They were also obtainable in Season 15 where the player collected 8 types of leaves using shears in adventures.


== Drops ==
When killed, pinto cows dropped:

0–2 Leather
1–3 Raw Beef (Cooked Beef if killed while on fire)
