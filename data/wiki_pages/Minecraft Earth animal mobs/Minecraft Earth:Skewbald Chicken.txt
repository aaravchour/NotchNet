The skewbald chicken was a skewbald variant of the chicken, found only in Minecraft Earth.


== Appearance ==
Skewbald chickens were a brown-and-white variant of the chicken. Its upper feathers surrounding its head and near the wings were marble-white in color, and its back feathers were darkish brown in color. Its beak and legs were saturated in color, somewhat resembling fleshtone colors.


== Behavior ==

Skewbald chickens acted exactly the same as normal chickens, walking aimlessly, and avoiding lava.


== Obtaining ==
Skewbald chickens were an uncommon mob drop found in chicken tappables. They were also obtainable as a reward in Season 17.


== Drops ==
When skewbald chickens were killed, they dropped:

0-2 Feather
1 Raw Chicken (Cooked Chicken if killed while on fire)
