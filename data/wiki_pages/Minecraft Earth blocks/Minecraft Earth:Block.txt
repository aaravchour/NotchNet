There were several blocks exclusive to Minecraft Earth.


== Mud ==
Mud was a slow-moving fluid that slowed down entities. Muddy Pigs liked it.

		
			
			
		


== Buttercup & Pink Daisy ==
The Buttercup and Pink Daisy were yellow and pink flowers found growing on Mooblooms and Moolips.

		
			
			
		
		
			
			
		


== Rainbow Wool, Carpet & Bed ==
Rainbow Wool was a rainbow-colored wool obtained from Rainbow Sheep. It could be used to craft Rainbow Carpets and Rainbow Beds.

		
			
			
		
		
			
			
		
		
			
			
		


== Adventure Chests ==
Adventure Chests were blocks found in Adventures. Each variant contained a different amount of valuable loot.
