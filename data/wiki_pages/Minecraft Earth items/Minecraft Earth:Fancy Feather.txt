The fancy feather was a colorful variant of the feather found exclusively in Minecraft Earth.


== Obtaining ==
The fancy feather was obtainable by either killing a fancy chicken or from a fancy chicken via chicken tappables.


== Usage ==
Fancy feathers didn't have a use in the game, and only acted as miscellaneous items or decorations for builds.


== History ==


== Trivia ==
The fancy feathers were first found in the code in Minecraft Earth version 0.23.0 before its official reveal, with only its menu icon.
