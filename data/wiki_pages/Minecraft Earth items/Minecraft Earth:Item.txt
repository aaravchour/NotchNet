There were several items exclusive to Minecraft Earth.


== Bucket of Mud ==
The Bucket of Mud was a bucket that could be used to carry and place Mud.

		
			
			
		


== Fancy Feather ==
The Fancy Feather was a purple feather dropped by Fancy Chickens.

		
			
			
		


== Horn ==
The Horn was an item dropped by Horned Sheep.

		
			
			
		


== Ruby ==
The Ruby was an item used as currency in the Store.

		
			
			
		


== Adventure Crystals ==
Adventure Crystals were items used for creating Adventures. Each variant created a different level of adventure.
