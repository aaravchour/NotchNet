A magma cow is a magma variant of the cow that appears in some Minecraft Earth concept art.


== Appearance ==
The magma cow appears to be a dark gray cow with large horns and legs made out of magma, and orange-glowing eyes and nostrils. It also appears to have flaming hooves, and veins of magma on the underside of its body.
