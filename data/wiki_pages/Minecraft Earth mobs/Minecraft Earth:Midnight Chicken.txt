A midnight chicken was a passive mob and a dark-colored variant of the chicken found in Minecraft Earth.


== Appearance ==
Midnight chickens were a dark-colored variant of the chicken. Their plumage was dark-bluish in color, making it reminiscent of coal or obsidian. Their beak was dark in color with a gray stripe running across it. They also possessed dark-colored legs.


== Behavior ==

Midnight chickens had the same AI behavior as ordinary chickens. They wandered aimlessly, avoiding lava and cliffs that were enough to cause fall damage.


== Obtaining ==
Midnight chickens were a rare mob drop from chicken tappables, with a 4.6% chance to get one. They were also obtainable as a reward in Season 17.


== Drops ==
When killed, midnight chickens dropped:

0-2 Feather
1 Raw Chicken (Cooked Chicken if killed while on fire)
