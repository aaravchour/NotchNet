The mooboom was an explosive variant of the cow that appeared in Minecraft Earth concept art.


== Appearance ==
The mooboom appears to be a cow made out of TNT, with dynamite sticks for horns, a fuse for a tail, and the letters "TNT" written on its sides. Some of the concept sketches depict it being ignited and exploding.
