The pink footed pig was a dark-colored variant of the pig with pink feet found exclusively in Minecraft Earth.


== Appearance ==
Pink footed pigs were a dark-colored variant of the pig. They possessed a pink nose, and their upper body and skin were dark in color, with a small light-gray patch on the left side of their belly. Its feet were light pink in color, contrasting the rest of its body, and were separated by light-gray patches of skin found on its knees. It had dark ears and light pink with a bit of cyan muzzle underneath its nose.


== Behavior ==

Like the average pig, pink footed pigs had the same AI behavior, wandering aimlessly, dodging lava and cliffs.


== Obtaining ==
Pink footed pigs were obtainable from pig tappables, with a 6.5% chance of getting it, and were of uncommon rarity. It was also previously obtainable as a reward in Season 7.


== Drops ==
When killed, pink footed pigs dropped:

1-3 Raw Porkchop (Cooked Porkchop if killed while on fire)
