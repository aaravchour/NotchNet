A redthorn is an obstacle that slows down and damages entities in Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Redthorn can be found in badlands and jungle biomes. They spawn in twisting lines.


== Usage ==
When an entity move into redthorn its movement speed and jump height is reduced, and it takes small damage quickly over time.
