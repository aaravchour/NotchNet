A brute is a piglin within the Horde of the Bastion of Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==


== Drops ==
Brutes drop lapis.


== Behavior ==
Blade Swipe

Brutes swipe at their enemies with their dual blades.
Blade Spin

Brutes spin around with their dual blades to attack their enemies.
