A lava launcher is a piglin that has a cannon with a magma cube that shoots magma within Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Lava launchers occasionally spawn in Horde of the Spore attacks, in the Zombie Homestead when it is first occupied, and in Horde of the Spore large and boss piglin bases. No more than 2 usually spawn.


== Drops ==
Lava launcher drop lapis.


== Behavior ==


=== Attacks ===
Magma Launcher
Lava launchers have a large cannon on their back with a magma cube inside. They use this cannon to launch magma at their enemies.

Stomp Attack
Lava launchers also have a melee attack by stomping their front hooves at their enemies.
