A piglin builder is a piglin  within Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==


== Drops ==
Piglin builders drop lapis.


== Behavior ==


=== Attacks ===
Grenade Mortar
Piglin builders shoot a grenade from their backpack that does AOE damage.

Build
Piglin builders can construst Blaze Rod Towers and Piglin Pits.
