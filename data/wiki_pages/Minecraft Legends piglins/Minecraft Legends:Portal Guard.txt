A portal guard is a piglin that uses a flail to attack within Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Portal Guards spawn near the portal of a Piglin Base.


== Drops ==
Portal guards drop lapis.


== Behavior ==


=== Attacks ===
Melee Swipe 

The portal guard uses its blade in its left arm to swipe at enemies which knocks them back.
Flail Throw 

The portal guard aims its flail at it's targets then shoots it at them dealing damage, knockbacks and stuns enemies then slowly retracts it back and as it retracts it also deals damage and some knockback.
Flail Spin

The portal guard spins its flail in the air knocking back nearby enemies.
