A sporeback is a piglin  within the Horde of the Spore of Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==


== Drops ==
Sporebacks drop lapis and a piglin key.


== Behavior ==


=== Attacks ===
Mushroom Throw
Sporebacks pluck explosive mushrooms from their back before throwing it at their enemies.
