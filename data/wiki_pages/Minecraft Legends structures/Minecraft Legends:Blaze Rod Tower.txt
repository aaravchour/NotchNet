A blaze rod tower is a piglin-built structure within Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Blaze rod towers spawn in piglin bases and piglin outposts.


== Usage ==
Blaze rod towers are used by piglins to defend their outposts and bases by shooting blaze rods at the Hero and allied mobs.
