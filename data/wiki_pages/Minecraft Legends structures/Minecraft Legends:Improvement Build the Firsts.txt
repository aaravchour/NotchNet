The improvement build the firsts is an improvement structure within Minecraft Legends. 


== Spawning ==


== Behavior ==
The improvement build the firsts allows you to summon ancient golems, such as the first of diorite, the first of oak, and the first of brick, each of which has a special capability to aid the player
