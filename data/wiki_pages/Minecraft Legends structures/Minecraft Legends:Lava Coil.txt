A lava coil is a piglin structure that can be found within the various piglin outposts of Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Lava coils spawn in high level piglin outposts.


== Usage ==
When a lava coil is in a piglin outpost, it prevents the nether portal in that outpost from being damaged and gives it a glowing lava effect.
