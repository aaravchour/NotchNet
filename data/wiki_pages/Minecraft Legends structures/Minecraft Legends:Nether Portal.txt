A Nether Portal,  also known as a Portal and an Obsidian portal, is a piglin structure that can be found within the various piglin outposts of Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Nether Portals spawn within piglin bases.


== Usage ==
Nether Portals allow the piglins to travel from the nether to the overworld.
