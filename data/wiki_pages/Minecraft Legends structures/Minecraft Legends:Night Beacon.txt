The Night Beacon is a Piglin Base that generates once per campaign that spawns with one layer of walls around the Nether Portal with blaze rod towers on the inner layer and piglin pits on the outside.


== Structures ==
Nether Portal
Piglin Gate
Piglin Walls
Blaze Rod Tower
Nether Spreader
Piglin Pit
