A piglin cage is a piglin-built structure within Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Piglin cages spawn in piglin outposts.


== Usage ==
Piglin cages are used by piglins to trap mobs such as villagers, animals and allied mobs. They can be opened by the Hero to release the mobs trapped inside.
