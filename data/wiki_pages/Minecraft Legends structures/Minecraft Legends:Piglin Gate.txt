A piglin gate is a piglin-built structure within Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Piglin gates spawn in piglin outposts and piglin bases.


== Behavior ==
Piglin gates are used by piglin to both keep the Hero and allied units out of their base and for piglins to enter and exit the piglin bases and outposts.
