A piglin wall is a piglin-built structure within Minecraft Legends.


== Spawning ==
Piglin walls spawn in piglin outposts and piglin bases.


== Behavior ==
Piglin walls are used by the piglins to protect their structures, mainly the nether portal from the Hero and the allied units.
