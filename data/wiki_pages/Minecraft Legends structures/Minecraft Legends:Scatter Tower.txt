A scatter tower is a short range defensive structure in Minecraft Legends.


== Obtaining ==
Scatter towers can be placed by selecting them in the hotbar and using a build allay, and spending 35 wood, 35 stone, and 10 coal.


== Usage ==
Campaign
Scatter towers have short range but fire arrows faster and in a volley.
