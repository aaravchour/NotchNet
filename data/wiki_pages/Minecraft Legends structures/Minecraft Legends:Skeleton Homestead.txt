A skeleton homestead is an ally structure in Minecraft Legends.


== Description ==
The skeleton homestead is populated by large bone structures, and features the skeleton of a large dinosaur-like creature as its centre-piece.


== Spawning ==
Skeleton homesteads will spawn in one of the tundra biomes in the overworld.
