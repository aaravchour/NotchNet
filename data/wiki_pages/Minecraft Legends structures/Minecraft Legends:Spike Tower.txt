A spike tower is a piglin-built structure within Minecraft Legends. It attacks by throwing a large explosive spike at enemies.


== Spawning ==
Spike tower spawns in piglin bases.


== Usage ==
Piglins use the spike tower to defend piglin bases by shooting large, spiked projectiles that explodes into smaller pieces which explodes to damage mobs.
