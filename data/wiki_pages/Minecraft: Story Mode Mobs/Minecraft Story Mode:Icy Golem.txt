Icy golems are an icy cold golem variant that can be fought optionally in "Giant Consequences".


== Behavior ==
Icy golems can throw snowballs which can knock back the player. It is unknown if they possess a melee attack


== Appearance ==
Icy golems are a snowy variant of the iron golem created by Romeo. Their bodies are snow white and instead of vines, they have greyish cracks on their chest and grey lines on their arms. They also wear a carved pumpkin on their head.


== Episode Appearances ==
In "Giant Consequences", if Jesse goes with Radar and Lukas, they'll have to run and then fight three the Icy Golems, managing to push all of them into the waters

== Trivia ==
As mentioned by Eric Stirpe, whatever mob in Romeo's Icy Palace of Despair was called "icy [mob name here]."
Icy golems have some similarities with the snow golems from the base game
They both have a weakness for water.
They both wear a pumpkin for a head; however, unlike the snow golem, the icy golem's pumpkin head cannot be sheared.
