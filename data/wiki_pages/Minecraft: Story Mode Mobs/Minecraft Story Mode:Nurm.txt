Nurm is a main character in Minecraft: Story Mode - Season Two. He first appeared in "Hero in Residence" and is voiced by Mark Barbolak. He is the only villager that appeared in the game.


== Appearance ==
Nurm is a cartographer villager originally from Pur'gan-Gah valley. He wears a white robe, a green hat with a black stripe with a white feather stuck into it, and a gold belt and cuffs.


== Personality ==
Nurm does not talk much, but he is very intelligent, being "the genius behind all of (Jack's) fine maps", and can read the runes in the Sea Temple. Unlike Jack, Nurm believes in the existence of the Admin genuinely.
Nurm can get scared in dangerous situations, but he trusts Jack and is daring enough to go adventuring with him. As an exception, he does fight in "Jailhouse Block," when he uses two iron axes to fight hostile mobs. He is willing to sacrifice himself for others, shown in "Jailhouse Block" when he offers to stay behind in the prison so Lluna can leave.

== Trivia ==
Nurm is currently the only villager encountered in Minecraft: Story Mode, not counting witches.
Nurm and Jack own an emporium together. This may be referencing villagers' ability to trade items.
It is shown that he understands the runes in the Sea Temple.
Jack is be able to communicate with Nurm by listening to him speak through villagerese. Jack tends to translate his words for him as Jesse's gang can't understand the language and Nurm is unable speak their language.
However, in Jailhouse Block, when Nurm offers to stay behind, Jesse is able to understand him just fine.
It is confirmed by Eric Stirpe that Nurm's vocal cords only allow him to speak Villagerese.
Nurm was confirmed to be a cartographer villager.
In "Jailhouse Block," Nurm is shown backing away in fear from zombies, this is a reference to villagers in vanilla Minecraft fearing zombies.
Nurm originally lived in the valley of Pur'gan-Gah until Jack visited and decided to join him on his adventures.
