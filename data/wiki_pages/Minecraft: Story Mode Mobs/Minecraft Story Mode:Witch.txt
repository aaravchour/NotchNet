Witches are a hostile mob found in Minecraft: Story Mode with them only appearing in A Block and a Hard Place.


== Behavior ==
Like their base game counterparts, they attack players with potions upon being seen.


== Episodes ==
In "A Block and a Hard Place", a witch is seen in the swamp protecting her hut against Jesse. Later, another witch is seen alongside skeletons attacking the heroes. Another witch is also one on the maze wall in the Far Lands.
