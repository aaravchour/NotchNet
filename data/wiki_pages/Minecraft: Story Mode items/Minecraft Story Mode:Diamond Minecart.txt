The Diamond Minecart is an item possessed by DanTDM in Minecraft: Story Mode.


== Appearance ==
The Diamond Minecart is diamond color and uses the old minecart from pre-1.14 due to the game being made before it. It appears in DanTDM's inventory.


== Usage ==
Diamond Minecarts are used to ride like a normal minecart, though its only used by DanTDM.
