The Crab is a mob proposed in the 2023 Mob Vote & Minecraft Live 2023. The Mob was in 2nd place in October 15th, 2023


== Spawning ==
Crabs spawn in mangrove swamps. 


== Drops ==
Somehow, Crabs drop claws. They are likely not killed, but can be farmed without dying.


== Behavior ==
Crabs can survive on land or water, like Axolotls and Dolphins, but probably last longer. They can also climb vines.
