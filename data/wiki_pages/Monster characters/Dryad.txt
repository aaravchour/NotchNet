Dryads are mythological monsters, two of them were seen with chimera in the Minecraft Mini-Series. Two dryads first appeared in episode 5 of the second season.


== Appearance ==
Dryads are female humanoid creatures, they have wings and are green, resembling plants.


== Personality ==
The dryads have a passion for acting. They like "stagecraft".


== Trivia ==
Dryads in Greek mythology are spirits who live in trees and take the form of a beautiful young woman to protect nature.
The dryad in the Minecraft Mini-Series is based on the "Dryad" from Mattel which in turn was based on a skin with the same name in the Greek Mythology Mash-up pack.
