The Great Bungus, also known as the Powerful Bungus, and Bungus, was the leader of the largest Piglin clan within The Nether of Minecraft Legends: Return of the Piglins. His name was mistaken as Great Fungus and Fungus by Farnum due to the language barrier.


== Appearance ==
It is currently unknown as to what the Great Bungus looks like other than a piglin.
