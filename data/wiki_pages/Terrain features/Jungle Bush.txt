Jungle bush is a variant of jungle trees that generates in jungle biomes and cannot be grown by jungle saplings. It is one of the rarest plants in the game.


== Generation ==
Jungle bush generates as a jungle log surrounded by oak leaves. Unlike jungle trees, jungle bushes can generate in all jungle biome variants.
