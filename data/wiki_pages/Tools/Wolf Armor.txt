== Usage ==

Using wolf armor on a tamed wolf will cause the wolf to equip it, in a similar manner to horse armor. While a wolf is using wolf armor, it gains 11 ( Ã— 5.5) armor points. In order to remove armor from the wolf, the player has to right click the wolf using shears, which grants the "Shear Brilliance" achievement.


=== Dyeing wolf armor ===
Wolf armor can be dyed using the crafting table. Similar to dying of leather armor, several colors can be used at once to create various shades.
