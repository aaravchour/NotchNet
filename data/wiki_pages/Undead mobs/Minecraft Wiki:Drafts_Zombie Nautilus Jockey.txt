A zombie nautilus jockey is an upcoming undead hostile jockey that will be added in Mounts of Mayhemâ€Œ. It consist of a drowned riding a zombie nautilus.


== Spawning ==
A drowned wielding a trident has a 50% chance of spawning as a zombie nautilus jockey.


== Behavior ==
When ridden by a drowned, it controls the zombie nautilus, making the zombie nautilus hostile.


=== As an Undead Mob ===
Like any undead mobs:

Are damaged by Instant Health and healed by Instant Damage;
Are not affected by Regeneration and Poison;
Take extra damage from Smite enchantment;
Are not targeted by the wither.
Causes an armadillo to hide in its shell.
Since they spawn underwater, they are not affected by sunlight.
