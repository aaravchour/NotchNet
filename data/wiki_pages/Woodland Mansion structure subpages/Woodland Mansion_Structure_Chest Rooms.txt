There are two sizes of chest rooms. None of the chests have loot.
One vindicator spawns in each room.

Small Chest Room (1x2_a9)
The oak slabs on layers 3, 5, and 7 are upside down.

Large Chest Room (2x2_a2)
All of the flower pots have blue orchids in them.
All of the oak slabs are upside down, except for the ones in layer 1.
