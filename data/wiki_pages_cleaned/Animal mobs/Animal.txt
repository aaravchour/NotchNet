The term animal refers to a category of mobs and blocks that are mainly based on real life animals and mythical/made-up animals.

These mobs can be directly bred. Majority of them are required for the Two by Two advancement with the Trader Llama only present in the The Parrots and the Bats advancement.

These animals can be tamed by different ways. The Best Friends Forever advancement applies to them.
