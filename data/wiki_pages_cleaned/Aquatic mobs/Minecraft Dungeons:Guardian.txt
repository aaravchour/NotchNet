A guardian is a hostile mob that can be found within the Hidden Depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

Guardians appear similar to guardians from the original game, the only difference being that they are darker in color.

Coral Rise (Ambush)
Abyssal Monument
Radiant Ravine

Laser targeting
Guardians aim their laser at the hero, the same as guardians within the original game.

Laser attack
Guardians shoot a green laser at the hero which does continuous damage, but can be blocked by hiding behind a solid surface.

Spike attack
Guardians also have a rare spike attack.
