Deepslate tiles are a decorative variant of deepslate.
Cracked deepslate tiles are a cracked variant of deepslate.

Deepslate tiles and cracked deepslate tiles generate naturally in ancient cities.

Deepslate tiles can be obtained only by mining it with a pickaxe. When mined using any other tool, it drops nothing. Mining deepslate tiles takes longer than mining deepslate.

Deepslate Tiles and its slab, stair and wall variants can also be obtained directly from stonecutting Deepslate Bricks or Cobbled Deepslate.
