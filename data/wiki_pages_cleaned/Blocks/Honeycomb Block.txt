Honeycomb blocks are decorative blocks crafted from honeycombs.

Honeycomb blocks can be broken using any tool, and always drop as an item.

Honeycomb blocks can be placed under note blocks to produce flute sounds.

Issues relating to "Honeycomb Block" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.

While real-life honeycombs are based on a hexagonal tiling, the honeycomb block's texture is more akin to a square tiling, likely to fit with the square theme of the game.
This makes the square tiling the second regular polyhedron to be represented in Minecraft, after the cube.
