Bleach is an item used to dye things white. It is a compound, as it is made using a lab table.

Bleach is not obtainable in the creative inventory or /give. The only way to obtain it is using a lab table.

 There is an unused bleach texture in the compounds folder of the chemistry resource pack, which is slightly different from the one used in the game.

Issues relating to "Bleach" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.
