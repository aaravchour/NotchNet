Breath of the Nautilus is an upcoming status effect that will be added in Mounts of Mayhem. It only activates when a player mounts a nautilus or zombie nautilus, which freezes the oxygen bar, preventing a player from drowning.

The following mobs are immune to "Breath of the Nautilus":

Wither
Ender Dragon

Issues relating to "Breath of the Nautilus" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.
