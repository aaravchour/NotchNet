Sharing is a joke effect from Java Edition 15w14a which replaces the data value of the weakness effect.

Sharing causes mobs to drop random items in a random amount of time. The items are:

Bread
Coal
Diamonds
Gold Ingots
Iron Ingots
Lapis Lazuli
Melon Seeds
Pumpkin Seeds
Redstone
Saddles
The effect can be removed by drinking milk.
