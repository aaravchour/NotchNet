A bush is a natural decorative plant added in Spring to Life. Like short grass, It will adapt different shades of green depending on the biome.

Bushes can only be dropped as an item using shears or any tool enchanted with Silk Touch. Otherwise, it drops nothing.

Bushes naturally generate in the following biomes (at small patches):

Birch Forest
Old Growth Birch Forest
Forest
Plains
Windswept Hills
Windswept Gravelly Hills
Windswept Forest
Shores of River and Frozen River

Using a Bone Meal on a bush grows another bush on neighboring blocks.

Like grass, bushes adapt different shades of green, depending on the biome

Bushes can be placed on the following blocks:

Grass Block
Mycelium
Podzol
Dirt
Coarse Dirt
Rooted Dirt
Farmland
Mud
Muddy Mangrove Roots
Moss Block
Pale Moss Block
In Bedrock Edition, it is possible to snowlog bushes.

Using a bush on a composter has a 30% chance of raising a compost level by 1.

Issues relating to "Bush" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.
