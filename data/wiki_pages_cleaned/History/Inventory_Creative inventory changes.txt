This page describes the history of the layout and availablity of blocks and items in the Creative Inventory.

In 22w42a, with the overhaul of the Creative inventory, numerous "bugs" were fixed. The list of bug reports that were resolved with the changes in 22w42a are as follows below:

Moved netherite ingot to come after netherite scrap in the Creative inventory instead of before.
Grouped nether wart blocks to be right after leaves in the Natural Blocks tab.
Grouped torches and soul torches together in the Functional Blocks tab.
Grouped obsidian and crying obsidian together in the Natural Blocks tab.
Grouped polished blackstone and basalt with other polished blocks in the Building Blocks tab.
Grouped shroomlight and nether wart blocks together in the Natural Blocks tab.
Grouped blackstone stairs and slabs with other stone stairs and slabs in the Building Blocks tab.
Grouped beds and respawn anchors together in the Functional Blocks tab.
Grouped dripstone and pointed dripstone together in the Natural Blocks tab.
Grouped moss block with dirt in the Natural Blocks tab.
Grouped block of emerald with other ore blocks in the Building Blocks tab.
Grouped frogspawn and turtle egg to be next to each other in the Natural Blocks tab.
Grouped muddy mangrove roots to be near mud blocks in the Natural Blocks tab.

Restored water bottle, awkward, thick, and mundane potion variants; of regular, splash, and lingering potions; to the Creative inventory; after their removal in 22w42a.
Grouped vines with weeping vines and twisting vines together in the Natural Blocks tab.
Moved netherrack to be next to nylium blocks in the Natural Blocks tab.
Added milk buckets to the Consumables tabs in the Creative inventory.
Added nether wart to the Natural Blocks tab in the Creative inventory.
Added bone meal to the Tools & Utilities tab in the Creative inventory.
