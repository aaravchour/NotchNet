Armadillo scutes are items that armadillos drop when brushed. They can be used to craft wolf armor which can be put on wolves.

Armadillos drop armadillo scutes when they are brushed.

Armadillo scutes can be crafted into wolf armor.

Armadillo scutes can be used to repair wolf armor.
