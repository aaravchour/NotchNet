A bottle o' enchanting is a throwable item that releases experience orbs on impact.

Master-level cleric villagers sell bottles o' enchanting for 3 emeralds as part of their trade.

A bottle o' enchanting can be thrown by pressing use. On impact, it drops experience orbs worth 311 (average 7.0) and sends out blue particles.

Java Edition:
Bottles o' enchanting use the Friendly Creatures sound category for entity-dependent sound events.
