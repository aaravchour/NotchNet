Magma cream is an item used in brewing to create potions of Fire Resistance, and to manually craft magma blocks.

All but tiny-sized magma cubes drop 01 magma cream. The maximum amount of magma cream is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 4 with Looting III.
