Sugar is a food ingredient and brewing ingredient made from sugar canes.

Witches have a chance of dropping 02 sugar upon death. This is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 05 sugar.

Sugar can be created from its base elements, using the compound creator.

Sugar can be fed to horses to heal 1, speed growth by 30 seconds, and increase taming probability by 3%.
