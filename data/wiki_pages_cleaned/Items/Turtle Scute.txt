Turtle Scutes are items that baby turtles drop when they grow into adults. They can be used to craft turtle shells which can be used to brew the potion of the Turtle Master.

Baby turtles drop a single turtle scute when they grow up into adults, which is the only way possible to obtain the item.

Turtle scutes can be crafted into turtle shells.

Turtle scutes are the repair items for the turtle armor material, and thus can be used to repair turtle shells in an anvil.

Expert-level cleric villagers have a 23 chance to buy 4 turtle scutes for an emerald as part of their trades.
Expert-level cleric villagers have a 50% chance to buy 4 turtle scutes for an emerald as part of their seventh trades.
Expert-level leatherworker villagers buy 4 turtle scutes for an emerald as part of their trades.
