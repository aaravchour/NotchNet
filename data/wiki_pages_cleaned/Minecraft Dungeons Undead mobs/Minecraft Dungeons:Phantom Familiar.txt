The phantom familiar is a cosmetic pet phantom variant that can be found within the Spooky Fall 2022 event of Minecraft Dungeons.

The phantom familiar is a bright red phantom variant which also possesses bright blue eyes. The phantom familiar resembles the red phantom variant which was made during its development, though it is unknown if it was intended or a mere coincidence.

Tower (Spooky Fall 2022)

Unlike regular phantoms, the phantom familiar simply follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
