The Axolotl (leucistic) is a cosmetic pet and axolotl variant that can be unlocked as a reward in the Luminous Night seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.

The Axolotl (Leucistic) is a common variant of the axolotl, with light pinkish skin, gills, limbs, and tail. 

Adventure Hub

The Axolotl (Leucistic) follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
