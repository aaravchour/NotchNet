A hoglin is a hostile mob  that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

Hoglins are a lot smaller than they are within the original game.

Nether Wastes
Warped Forest
Crimson Forest
Soul Sand Valley

Basic attack
Hoglins attack heroes by hitting them with their tusks.

Rush
Hoglins charge at heroes and fling them into the air if they are to far for the basic attack.

In the game files, hoglins are referred to internally by the name Hoglin.
In some of the game files, the rush attack is referred to internally by the name ChargeStart.

Hoglins and piglins don't seem to attack each other unlike within the original game.
Hoglins sound similar to baby hoglins in the the base game since they sound high pitched.
Hoglins are the main source of pork within flames of the nether.
