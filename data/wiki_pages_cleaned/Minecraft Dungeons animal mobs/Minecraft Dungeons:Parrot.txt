The parrot is a pet mob found in Minecraft Dungeons. Statues of parrots are found throughout the Dingy Jungle mission, scattered about.

The parrot resembles the Parrot exactly as it does in the original game. It can be seen in the ending cinematic of the Broken Citadel mission.
