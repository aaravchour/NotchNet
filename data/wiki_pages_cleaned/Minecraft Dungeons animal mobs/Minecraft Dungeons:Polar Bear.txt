Not to be confused with Polar Bear, from Minecraft

A polar bear is a neutral animal that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

Frosted Fjord
Lost Settlement

Polar bears are usually found wandering around the mission.

Attack
They attack heroes when provoked, where they charge at them and maul them with their claws.

Polar bears are the first neutral mobs added to Minecraft Dungeons.
The polar bear is used as an obtainable pet in the arcade version of the game.
There are unused baby polar bear sounds in the game files.
