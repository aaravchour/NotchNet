The ruby baby turtle is a cosmetic baby turtle variant that can be unlocked as a reward in the Cloudy Climb seasonal adevnture of Minecraft Dungeons.

The ruby baby turtle is a ruby-colored turtle variant possessing dark ruby skin and shell. Its ruby shell is lined with three beige-colored stripes and its sides of its shell are of the same color. It possesses a beige underbelly, lower jaw, underside of its front flippers, and forehead. It lastly has grey eyes.

Adventure Hub

The ruby baby turtle is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
