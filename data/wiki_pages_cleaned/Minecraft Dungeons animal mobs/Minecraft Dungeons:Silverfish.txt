A silverfish is a small hostile arthropod that can be found within the Echoing Void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

Silverfish are small silvery bug-like mobs, possessing fuzzy thorns on their back and having segmented bodies. They have black eyes and a black tail tip.

Silverfish quickly scuttle toward a target to bite them, inflicting a small amount of damage, similarly to how they behave within the original game.
