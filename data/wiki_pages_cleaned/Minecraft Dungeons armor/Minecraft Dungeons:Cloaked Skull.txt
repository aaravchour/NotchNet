The cloaked skull is a unique ghostly armor variant that could be found within the Spooky Fall 2022 event of Minecraft Dungeons.

The cloaked skull functions indetically to the ghost kindler.

+X health
Briefly gain Ghost Form when rolling
30% chance to negate damage
Spawns fire on roll (Fire Trail tier I)
