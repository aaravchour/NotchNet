Scale mail is an armor set that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the highland armor.

A reaver is an unseen illager, referred to as the infamous reavers of the sea, within the old description of scale mail armor.

Not much is known about their appearance other than that they mostly wear scale mail and possibly are adept at anything related to the sea.

Issues relating to "Scale Mail" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.

A set of scale mail armor can be seen in the Official Dungeons Banner and the Rise of The Arch-Illager cover with Hex wearing it and wielding a pair of daggers.
