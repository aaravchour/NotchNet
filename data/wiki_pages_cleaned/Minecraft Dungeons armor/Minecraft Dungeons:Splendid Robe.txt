An illager warrior is an unseen illager, referred to as the special protectors of the Arch-Illager within the description of the splendid robe.

Not much is known about their appearance other than that they mostly wear the splendid robe, and possibly resemble an evoker.

In the game files, the splendid robe is referred to internally by the name DragonRobe.
The name of the texture has a typo, with it being referred to as the T_DraognRobe.
