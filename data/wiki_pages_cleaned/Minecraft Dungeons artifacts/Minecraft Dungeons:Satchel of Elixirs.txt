The satchel of elixirs is an artifact that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Coral Rise (Adventure)
Radiant Ravine
Merchants

Luxury Merchant

The satchel of elixirs allows heroes to brew two random potions when activated, which can help in tight situations. The potions brewed will be either a strength potion, a swiftness potion, or a shadow brew. Potions can be brewed in all of the DLC missions except for the Flames of the Nether.

Oakwood Brew can be brewed in:
Dingy Jungle
Overgrown Temple
Panda Plateau
Sweet Brew can be brewed in:
Frosted Fjord
Lone Fortress
Lost Settlement
Dense Brew can be brewed in:
Windswept Peaks
Gale Sanctum
Colossal Rampart
Potion of Water Breathing can be brewed in:
Coral Rise
Abyssal Monument
Radiant Ravine
Burning Brew can be brewed in:
The Stronghold
End Wilds
Broken Citadel

25 second cooldown
