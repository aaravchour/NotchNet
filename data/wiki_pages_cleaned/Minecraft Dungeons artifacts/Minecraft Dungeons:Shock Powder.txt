The shock powder is an artifact that can be used to stun nearby enemies for a certain amount of seconds, depending on the Power Level of the item. As stated in the description for this item, it is useful for escaping difficult situations, such as large crowds of enemies. It has a cooldown of 15 seconds, which can be shortened with certain armor sets and the Cool Down enchantment.
