An anchor is a melee weapon that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Anchors can be used to absorb knockback and deal heavy damage to enemies. It's unique variant is called the encrusted anchor.

The anchor is a very large shiny metallic anchor that is wielded by the hero with just one hand. 

The item description states that within the past, legendary seafaring warriors wielded the anchor in battle, following an ancient tradition.
