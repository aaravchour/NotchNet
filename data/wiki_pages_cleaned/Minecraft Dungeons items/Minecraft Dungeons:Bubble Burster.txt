The bubble burster is a unique bubble bow variant that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

The bubble burster can be used to trap mobs in bubbles via charged shots that eventually defeat them. The bubble burster has a unique exclusive enchantment which allows charged shots to bounce off multiple targets.
