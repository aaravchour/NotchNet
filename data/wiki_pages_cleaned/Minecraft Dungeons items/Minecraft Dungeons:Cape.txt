The term cape refers to cosmetic equipment that provides no functional benefits to the hero. All available cosmetics can be equipped from the cosmetics page  of the inventory.

Capes appear on the back on a hero and rise during hero movement. Hero capes, sinister capes, and birthday capes are the only ones currently available. The hero cape is available with the Hero Pass and the Hero Edition of the game. The sinister cape became available for all heroes on 2/24/2021. The birthday cape was obtainable during the Anniversary Event.
