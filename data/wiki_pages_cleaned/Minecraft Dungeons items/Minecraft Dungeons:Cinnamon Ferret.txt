The cinnamon ferret is a cosmetic pet mob that can be unlocked as a reward in the Fauna Faire seasonal adventure of Minecraft Dungeons.

The cinnamon ferret is a long small mob with a long body with cinnamon fur, as well as white fur on its underbelly, paws, and black fur on the tip of its tail. It has small ears.

Adventure Hub

The cinnamon ferret follows heroes around. It is purely cosmetic and does not attack any mobs.
