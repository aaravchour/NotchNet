The eye of the guardian is an artifact that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

Missions
Coral Rise (Apocalypse)
Abyssal Monument
Merchants
Village Merchant
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant

Similar to the corrupted beacon, this artifact fires a continuous laser beam at the direction the hero is facing. When used, the artifact charges up for around a second before firing a beam similar to that of a guardian's laser. While firing, turning speed is limited.

X artifact damage per second
22 second cooldown
