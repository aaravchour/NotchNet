The fangs of frost are a unique daggers variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

Fangs of frost possess low power, high speed, and low area, and have a continuous attack of six sideways slashes.

In the game files, the fangs of frost are referred to internally by the name frost knives.
In the sound files, the fangs of frost are referred to internally by the name ice knives.
