The fireworks arrow is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

When the fireworks arrow is used, an firework is fired that explodes in a radius similar to TNT upon impact with blocks or mobs.

When activated, the fireworks arrow puts one firework arrow in the hero's quiver.
