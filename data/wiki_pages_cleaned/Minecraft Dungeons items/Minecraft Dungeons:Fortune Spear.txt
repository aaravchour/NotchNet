The fortune spear is a  unique spear variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The fortune spear possesses moderate power, low speed, and high range, and has a combo consisting of three thrusts. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, the third deals the maximum melee damage.

In the game files, the fortune spear is referred to internally by the name RoyalSpear.
