Guard's armor is an armor set that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the unobtainable ender armor.

In the game files, guard's armor is referred to internally by the name CowardsArmor, and has chicken noises in its sounds, but it does not come with the cowardice enchantment.
