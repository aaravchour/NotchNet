The master's bow is a unique hunting bow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The Master's Bow is a tall bow, it has a light brown colour with a black handle and white string. The bow also features sharp spikes coming out of the front. the colour of the bow is similar to the regular Bow too, however they are separate items.
