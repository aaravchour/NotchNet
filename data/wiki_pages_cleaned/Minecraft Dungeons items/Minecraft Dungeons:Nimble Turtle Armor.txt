The nimble turtle armor is a unique turtle armor variant that can be found within the hidden depths DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

Missions
Coral Rise
Radiant Ravine
Tower
Merchants
Gift Wrapper
Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant
Piglin Merchant
Ancients
Vengeful Mariner

The nimble turtle armor is rather bulky but useful due to the healing boost and speed boost after hit, allowing quick escapes from mobs.

+X health
35% damage reduction
25% healing boost
Boost speed after hit (Rush tier I)
