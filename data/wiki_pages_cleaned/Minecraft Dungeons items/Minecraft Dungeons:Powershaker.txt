The powershaker is an artifact that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

Missions
Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

Warped Forest
Merchants

Mystery Merchant
Luxury Merchant

Using the power shaker artifact applies the effect Party Starter, which causes the hero's next melee attacks to have a chance to detonate mobs, akin to the Exploding enchantment.

X artifact damage
10 second cooldown

In the game files, the powershaker is referred to internally by the name MobMasher and the name Powershake.
