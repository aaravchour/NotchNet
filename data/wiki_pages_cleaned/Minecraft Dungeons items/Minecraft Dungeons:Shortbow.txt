A shortbow is a ranged weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the Mechanical Shortbow, the Purple Storm, and the Love Spell Bow.

Shortbows possess low power, high speed, and a very large ammo supply. The quiver starts with 160 arrows, normal arrow bundles give 80 arrows, supply chest bundles give another 160 arrows, and Surprise Gift bundles give 40 arrows.
