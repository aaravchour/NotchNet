The shrieking crossbow is a unique veiled crossbow variant that could be found within the Spooky Fall 2022 event of Minecraft Dungeons.

Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall 2022 (ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper

The shrieking crossbow functions identically to the veilded crossbow.

X ranged damage
Chance of Shadow Form on kills (Shadow Shot)
First shot won't break Shadow Form (Shadow Barb)

Quiver: 53
Fire Rate: 1.67 shots per second
