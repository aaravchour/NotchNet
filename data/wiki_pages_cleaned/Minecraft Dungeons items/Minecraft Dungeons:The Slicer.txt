The slicer is a unique crossbow variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The slicer possesses high power, low speed, and a moderate ammo supply. The quiver starts with 53 arrows, normal arrow bundles give 26 arrows, supply chest bundles give another 53 arrows, and Surprise Gift bundles give 13 arrows.

X ranged damage
Faster projectiles

Fire Rate: 1 shot per second
