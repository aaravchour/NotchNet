The thundering quiver is an artifact that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

The item description mentions that the thundering quiver and thundering arrows are made out of copper, which is a reference to how copper conducts electricity.

The thundering quiver grants the hero 5 thundering arrows when activated. Upon hitting an enemy with a thundering arrow lightning will spark and damage other nearby enemies. 
The damage done by the thundering quiver is increase by the lightning focus enchantment.

A thundering arrow is an arrow with the thundering effect that can be summoned within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

In the game files, the thundering quiver is referred to internally by the name ThunderingQuiver.
In the game files, thundering arrows are referred to internally by the name ThunderingArrow.
