The vine whip is a unique whip variant that can be found within the jungle awakens DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

The vine whip possesses high power, low speed, and low area, and attacks only with one lash.

X melee damage
Longer melee reach
Inflicts poison (Jungle Poison)

In the game files, the vine whip is referred to internally by the names PoisonWhip,Poison_Whip, and Whip_Unique1.
