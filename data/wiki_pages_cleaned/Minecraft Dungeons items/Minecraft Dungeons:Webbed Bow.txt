The webbed bow is a unique snow bow variant that can be found within the various spookier fall missions of Minecraft Dungeons. It has identical abilities to the winter's touch.

Missions
Tower
Events
Spookier Fall (ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper

The webbed bow has moderate power, speed, and ammo. Similar to Scatter Crossbow, each arrow considered individually. Meaning each arrows had chance to trigger enchantments instead of only arrow in the middle triggers.

X ranged damage
Fires 3 arrows when charged
Freezes on impact

Quiver: 80
Charge Multiplier: 2.5
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second

In the game files, the webbed bow is referred to internally by the name SlowBow_Spooky2.

Issues relating to "Webbed Bow" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.

The Webbed Bow is the first event item to be a reskin of a DLC item
