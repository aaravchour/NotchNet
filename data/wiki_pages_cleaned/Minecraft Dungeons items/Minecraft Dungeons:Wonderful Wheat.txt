The wonderful wheat is an artifact that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The wonderful wheat has the appearance of a lot of wheat stalks wrapped together near the bottom by a red object.

A pet llama is a summonable mob that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. They aid heroes by spitting at hostile mobs to deal damage.

Llamas have the appearance of brown trader llamas from the original game. They have two variants. One has a blue carpet and scarf while the other has a red carpet and scarf.

Llamas follow the hero around and defend them by attacking mobs

Basic attack spit
Llamas spit at hostile mobs, which deals damage upon contact, this behavior is different from llamas within the original game, where they attack only when provoked.

Damage resistance
All damage taken by llamas is halved (0.5x/50%).
