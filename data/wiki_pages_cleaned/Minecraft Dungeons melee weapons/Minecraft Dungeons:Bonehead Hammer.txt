The bonehead hammer is a unique great hammer variant that could be found within the Spooky Fall 2022 event of Minecraft Dungeons.

Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall 2022 (ended)
Merchants

The bonehead hammer functions identically to the hammer of gravity.

X melee damage
Great splash
Pulls in enemies (Gravity tier I)

In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.
