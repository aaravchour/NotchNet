The broadsword is a unique claymore variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The Broadsword has a wide, double edged blade, light bluish handguard and pommel and a black hilt.

The broadsword possesses high power, low speed, and moderate range, and has a combo consisting of a slash, thrust, and a slam. The slash deals the weapon's minimum melee damage, the thrust does about 20% more damage, and the slam does the weapon's maximum melee damage.
