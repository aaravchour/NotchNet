The great axeblade is a unique claymore variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The great axeblade seems to be a combination of the hawkbrand and a double axe.

The great axeblade possesses high power, very low speed, and moderate area, and has a combo consisting of a slash, thrust, and a slam.
The slash deals the weapon's minimum melee damage, the thrust does about 20% more damage, and the slam does the weapon's maximum melee damage.
