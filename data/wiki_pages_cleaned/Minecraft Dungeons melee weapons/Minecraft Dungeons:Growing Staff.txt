The growing staff is a  unique battlestaff variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The growing staff possesses moderate power, low speed, and moderate range. Its attack combo consists of two side swings, one wide swing, one slam, two stabbing moves, two side twirls, one spin attack, and one very powerful slam. Its combo resets after 0.5 seconds, unlike most other weapons that reset their combos after three seconds.

Issues relating to "Growing Staff" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.

The growing staff is most likely based off of the Ruyi Jingu Bang, the magical size-changing staff of the Monkey King Sun Wukong in the Chinese novel, Journey to the West.
