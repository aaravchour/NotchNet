The hawkbrand is a unique sword variant that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons.

The item description states that the hawkbrand is a legendary sword wielded by proven warriors.

The hawkbrand possesses moderate power, low speed, and low range. It have a combo consisting of two side slashes and a thrust. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, while the third, the thrust, deals the maximum melee damage.
