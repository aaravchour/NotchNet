The heartstealer possesses high power, low speed, and moderate range, and has a combo consisting of a slash, thrust, and a slam. The slash deals the weapon's minimum melee damage, the thrust does about 20% more damage, and the slam does the weapon's maximum melee damage.

In-game, damage numbers can vary even in the same power level, and may not be exactly as seen below.

The distinguished general is an unseen illager mentioned in the description of the heartstealer.

Not much is known about their appearance other than it wielded the heartstealer.
There is also a statue in the Highblock Halls that wears dark armor and wields a heartstealer-like sword.

The item description states that the distinguished general obtained the heartstealer after they conquered the squid coast.

In the game files, the heartstealer is referred to by the name CrimsonCore.
