A katana is a melee weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the dark katana and the master's katana.

Katanas possess high power, low speed, and moderate range, and have combo attacks consisting of two side slashes, and an upwards slash. The side slashes deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, and the upward slash deals the weapon's maximum melee damage.
