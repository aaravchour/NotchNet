The mechanized sawblade is a unique broken sawblade variant that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

The mechanized sawblade possesses low power, high speed, and low area. It has a combo consisting of rapid damage until overheating. The Mechanized Sawblade lasts longer before overheating than broken sawblades. The hero can manually end the combo by letting go of the attack button.
