The nightmare's bite possesses low power, moderate speed, and low range. It has a combo consisting of four alternating quick strikes, and two stronger strikes. The first four hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, the fifth does 100% more damage, and the sixth deals the maximum melee damage.
