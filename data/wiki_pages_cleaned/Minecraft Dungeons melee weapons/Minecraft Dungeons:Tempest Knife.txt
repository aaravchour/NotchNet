A tempest knife is a melee weapon that can be found within the Howling Peaks DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It has two unique variants, the resolute tempest knife and the chill gale knife.

Listed difficulties are considered to be the minimum difficulty the item is obtainable on unless stated otherwise.

A tempest knife possesses moderate power, moderate speed, and low area. It has a combo consisting of a thrust and two side slashes. The first two hits deal the weapon's minimum melee damage, while the third, the second slash, deals the maximum melee damage.
