The Starless Night is a unique variant of the Obsidian Claymore that can be found within the Echoing Void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

The starless night possesses above high power, low speed, and high area. Its combo consists of a slash which deals the weapon's minimum damage, and a slam that deals the weapon's maximum damage.
