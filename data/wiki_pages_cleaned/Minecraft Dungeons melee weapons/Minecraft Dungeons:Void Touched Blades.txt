Void touched blades are melee weapons that can be found within the echoing void DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is called the beginning and the end.

Void Touched Blades are double swords that seemingly resemble a pair of diamond swords from the original game, with the difference being that these blades possess a dark purple light around the edges with a darker tint upon the handguard, hilt and pommel.

Void touched blades possess moderate power, low speed, and low area. They have a combo consisting of three slashes, and one stronger spin attack. The first three hits deal the weapon's minimum damage, and the fourth hit deals the weapon's maximum damage.
