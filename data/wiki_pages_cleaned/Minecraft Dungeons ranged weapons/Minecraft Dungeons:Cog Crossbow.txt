A cog crossbow is a ranged weapon that can be found within the flames of the nether DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variant is the pride of the piglins

Holding down the fire button when using a cog crossbow rapidly fires between one and five arrows, known as a wind-up attack, after this it will fire normally at a slow speed.
