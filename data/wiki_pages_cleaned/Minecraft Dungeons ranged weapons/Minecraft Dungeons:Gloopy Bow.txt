The gloopy bow is a unique bubble bow variant that could be found within the Spooky Fall 2022 event of Minecraft Dungeons.

Missions
Tower
Events
Spooky Fall 2022 (Ended)
Merchants
Gift Wrapper

The gloopy bow functions identically to the bubble burster. 

X ranged damage
Bubble damage
Hits multiple targets when charged (Reliable Ricochet)

Charge Multipler: 2.5
Charge Time: 0.35 seconds
Quiver: 80
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second (uncharged)
