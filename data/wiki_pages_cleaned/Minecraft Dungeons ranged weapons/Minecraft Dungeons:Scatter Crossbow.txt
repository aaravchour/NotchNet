A scatter crossbow is a ranged weapon that can be found within the various missions of Minecraft Dungeons. Its unique variants are the harp crossbow and the lightning harp crossbow.

A scatter crossbow has moderate power, somewhat high speed, and moderate ammunition. It shoots three arrows at once, but they do not all travel in the same direction. The arrows can deal significant damage to a single enemy at close range.
