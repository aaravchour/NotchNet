A wind bow is  a ranged weapon that can be found within the howling peaks DLC of Minecraft Dungeons. It has a unique property when it fires fully charged arrows, known as "gale arrows". Its unique variants are the burst gale bow and the echo of the valley.

Charging a wind bow will turn the arrows into "gale arrows". Hitting an enemy with a gale arrow will cause a helix of wind to form around the hit enemy, and the wind will pull the enemy closer to the player.

In the game files, wind bows have a hidden enchantment referred to internally by the name WindBowEnchantment, which fully changed charged arrows into gale arrows.
