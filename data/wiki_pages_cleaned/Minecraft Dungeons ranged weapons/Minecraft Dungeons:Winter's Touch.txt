The winter's touch is a unique snow bow variant that can be found within the creeping winter DLC of Minecraft Dungeons.

The winter's touch has moderate power, speed, and ammo. Similar to Scatter Crossbow, each arrow considered individually. Meaning each arrows had chance to trigger enchantments instead of only arrow in the middle triggers.

X ranged damage
Freezes on impact
Fires 3 arrows when charged

Quiver: 80
Charge Multiplier: 2.5
Fire Rate: 2.2 shots per second

Some sounds for snow bows are found in the mob audio folder.
