The muddy foot rabbit was a variant of the rabbit with brown-colored feet and ears, found only in Minecraft Earth.

Muddy foot rabbits were a brown-tipped variant of the rabbit. It had a white body, with dark brown-tipped ears, feet, tail, and muzzle. It also had hazel eyes and a black nose. They are based off of the Californian Rabbit.

Muddy foot rabbits had the same behavior as normal rabbits, wandering around aimlessly, avoiding lava and cliffs.

Muddy foot rabbits were an uncommon mob drop found in grass and pond tappables, with it having a 0.7% chance of being obtained from both grass and pond tappables.

When killed, the muddy foot rabbits dropped:

01 Rabbit hide
01 Raw Rabbit (Cooked Rabbit if killed while on fire)
Rabbit's foot (10% chance of dropping)
