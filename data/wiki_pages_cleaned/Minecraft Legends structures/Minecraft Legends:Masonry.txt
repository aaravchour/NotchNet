A masonry is a structure that upgrades wooden structures to stone in Minecraft Legends.

Campaign
Masonries can be built using a build allay, which cost 50 wood, 100 stone and 200 iron.

PVP
Masonries cost 500 stone and 100 iron for players to build.

The masonry upgrades basic wood structures to stone, increasing their durability. These structures visually change over time to indicate the masonry is strengthening them. It can strengthen extendable walls, towers and gates.
