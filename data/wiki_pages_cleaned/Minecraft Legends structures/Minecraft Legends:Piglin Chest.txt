A piglin chest is a chest that grants resources within Minecraft Legends.

Piglins chest can be found randomly placed in piglin outposts.

Piglin chests can only be opened once a piglin key is obtained. When a piglin chest is opened, the resources within are obtained, which can include gather allays and build allays.

Issues relating to "Piglin Chest" are maintained on the bug tracker. Report issues there.
