A plank golem spawner is a spawner that summons a plank golem that helps the hero of Minecraft Legends.

Plank golem spawners can be placed by using a build allay, which costs 25 wood and 25 stone.

Plank golem spawners can be used to spawn plank golems by interacting with them and pressing the Spawn control. Plank golems cost one wood, one lapis, and take up one Flame of Creation to be spawned.
