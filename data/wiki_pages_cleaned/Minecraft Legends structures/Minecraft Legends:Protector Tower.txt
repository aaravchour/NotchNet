A protector tower is a structure that destroys nearby large enemy projectilles within Minecraft Legends. 

The protector tower can be placed by using a build allay, which will cost:

Campaign: 75 wood, 75 stone and 60 coal.
PVP: 450 stone and 200 iron.

Campaign
The protector tower destroys large enemy projectiles in a big area around it. It can only be upgraded by the masonry.
