A horse is a passive mob in Minecraft: Story Mode used to transport people to different places faster.

In "The Last Place You Look", the player can see the horses in a pen when Jesse's gang exit from the tunnel from Soren's Fortress.
In "A Block and a Hard Place", Jesse's gang use four horses to escape from the Wither Storm and travel to the Far Lands.
In the epilogue of "Hero in Residence", Lukas rides off on a horse to Beacontown.
In "Giant Consequences", Lukas's horse returns, and plays a role in the fight with the Admin.

A donkey is a passive mob in Minecraft: Story Mode used to transport people to different places faster. It only appeared physically in "The Order of the Stone" behind a fence and are briefly mentioned in "The Last Place You Look".

The white horse which Jesse rides has a unique texture unlike the fully white horses in vanilla Minecraft. It has a dark blue mane and tail, and light blue eyes.
The horses that appear in the episode "A Block and a Hard Place" have been tamed already.
They had a very brief appearance in the previous episode after Jesses Gang exited the stronghold using the minecarts. They were within fences seen to the left of the minecart exit.
Donkeys only appeared once physically, in "The Order of the Stone" behind a fence.
