Super TNT is an item possessed by Soren in Episode 3 of Season One.

Super TNT resembles normal TNT but its inner textures seem to glow like lava.

Super TNT is used to craft the formidi-bomb by combining it with 8 gunpowder in the crafting grid. According to Soren, putting super TNT near gunpowder is very risky, as the ingredients are very unstable when placed nearby.

The Super TNT's texture appears to be filled with lava, possibly giving it more explosive power.
It was believed that Super TNT was an alias for formidi-bomb, until Soren revealed that they are two different things.
Soren mentioned that the Super TNT was one of his uncraftable "impossible items".
It is possible he obtained it using the Command Block.
According to Soren, Super TNT is very unstable near gunpowder.
