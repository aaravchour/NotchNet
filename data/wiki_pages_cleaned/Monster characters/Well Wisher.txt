The Well Wisher is a undead vengeful spirit in the Minecraft Mini-Series. She first appeared in episode 3 as the main antagonist.

The Well Wisher is a floating female ghost. She has long black hair and her skin is a dull yellow-green. She wears a white tattered dress and a hat which resembles a well.

The Well Wisher is a vengeful aggressive monster. Not much of her personality is shown.

The Well Wisher in the Minecraft Mini-Series has a minifigure by Matel which is based on a skin with the same name in the "Campfire Tales" skin pack.
